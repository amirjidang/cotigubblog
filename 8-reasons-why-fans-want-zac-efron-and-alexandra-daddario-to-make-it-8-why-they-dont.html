    According to critics, the movie Baywatch could have been improved in several areas. But the talented cast that included Zac Efron and Alexandra Daddario still left plenty of people entertained.

    The film also made close to $200 million at the box office which means that it may not come as a surprise to hear that a sequel is already in the works. But Efron and Daddario may be "training" for that role this upcoming summer on the beach if the rumors are true that the two may be quickly becoming more than friends.

    There are plenty of reasons to root for the two to become an item, including potentially getting to see more of their flirtatious nature that has been put on display on Instagram. Not to mention the fact that if they do become an item, you'd be sure to see more of both of them. And after Channing and Jenna Tatum left you wondering if love can still exist in Hollywood, what better couple to cheer for than Efron and Daddario?

    But before you start shipping them too hard, there are also plenty of reasons why they wouldn't work and why they should both be hesitant. Including potential ramifications to the sequel. Which is a concern that becomes more real when you read about Efron's relationships with his former bed buddies.

    Efron's experience with Disney may lead you to believe in happy endings, so here are 7 reasons why Efron and Daddario should start shacking up but be warned because there are also 8 why they should just stay friends.

    "I date, but I'm really nervous around boys. I get very tentative."

    Okay, there's nothing wrong with that. Daddario must get approached on a regular basis, and provided Efron doesn't mind making the first move, things should be good to go. But wait, in an interview with Glamour magazine, Efron explained, "I think it's fine for girls to ask boys out. I actually prefer it." Uh oh, which one is going to jump out of their comfort zone?

    Yes - They Have a Cute Meeting Story

    One of the most anticipated movies of this past summer was Baywatch. Both Daddario and Efron deserve a lot of credit for the hype that was around the movie. Where they play on-screen lovers and quickly became close friends away from the screen.

    When talking about the opportunity to work with Zac Efron, Daddario described him as being "incredibly nice" during a discussion with Us Weekly. While Efron hasn't been as open about his first impressions of Daddario, if he's to live up to that description, we're sure he'd have nothing but amazing things to say about her. Though she also commented to People,

    "He had abs in places I didn't know you could have abs."

    If you love seeing couples fall in love on the set of a movie, which is how both parties had fallen in love previously before finding each other, than this ship is definitely for you.

    No - Zac has dated famous people and it hasn't gone well

    One of the biggest relationships of Zac Efron's life came when he was dating the model, Sami Miro for close to 2 years. The relationship ended in 2016 and Zac promptly made sure to scrub his social media profiles clean of any mention of her. Something he probably wouldn't have the luxury of doing for Daddario when you consider some of his social media posts are promoting Baywatch, and inevitably, Baywatch 2.

    When an insider for E! was asked on why the two went their separate ways, they stated,

    "It wasn't an easy breakup. Distance and work schedules got in the way with their relationship and Zac wanted to focus on his career."

    With both Efron and Daddario in the prime of their career, perhaps now is the worst time for them to start up a relationship.

    This includes former reports from the DailyMail that talked about Efron's habit of partying for several days straight and engaging in several substances that we're sure Daddario would want no part of.

    While Efron self-admitted himself to rehab and has since 2013, having an extra positive influence in his life in the form of Daddario is still something that is worth rooting for. Though to be fair, this could also backfire as Efron has also been spotted getting all up on the business of past potential partners including Michelle Rodriguez.

    When a relationship ends, it may be incredibly hard (and in many cases, not beneficial) to try and remain on close-terms with your partner.     But considering the film includes a relationship and flirty banter between Efron and Daddario's character, the idea of the two of them becoming more than friends away from the screen may make some fans plenty nervous.

    Especially if the relationship ends on awkward terms. And considering they haven't even started filming yet, that's very possible and is just added pressure to the relationship.

    That being said, if they aren't already engaging in some private time together, getting to spend a few months on the beach filming the sequel is sure to not hurt their chances.

    Zac also appears in a comedy starring Matthew McConaughey and Snoop Dogg titled The Beach Bum.

    Biography Timeline

    Allegra Miles is an American singer-songwriter who gained recognition after competing on season 18 of “The Voice” in 2020. Born on January 15, 2003, in St. John’s, Antigua and Barbuda, Miles moved to Florida with her family when she was ten. She started singing at a young age and honed her skills by performing at local events and open mics. Her appearance on “The Voice” earned her a large following and praise from the judges for her soulful voice and emotional performances.

    Obituaries from people all over the world started to pile up when they heard the news of Joyce Rodgers’s death. The celebrated evangelist and the author have passed away at the age of 65 on May 19. 

    As confirmed by the Twitter handle of the Church of God of Christ, she has died following a serious illness. However, her sickness remains a mystery as no details about her death have been surfaced until now. 

    Joyce Rodgers Illness and Obituary: Cause of Death

    Joyce Rodgers has died after suffering from an illness. Rodgers’s death has left everyone mourning. 

    It was already in light that the evangelist Joyce was in the hospital after being sick. Hence, people from all across the world were praying for her well-being. 

    However, the famous evangelist took her last breath on May 19 and died in the hospital. A woman of God and a fierce believer, she has saved thousands of people with her preaching.

    Joyce Rodgers doesn’t have a Wikipedia bio of her own. 

    However, she does have a few personal websites where she helped people in need. But for now, those websites are not in service. 

    Apart from being an evangelist, Joyce was also an author and motivational speaker. She was a conference leader and became quite an important aspect of COGIC youth over the years.

    Jennifer Lopez Says She’s Never Felt More Beautiful In A Relationship

    "I’m finally at the point in my life where I love every part of myself unapologetically,” she explained. “Every part of me, my body, my voice, my choices, even, like, my mistakes. All of it made me who I am and got me to where I am today.”

    J.Lo went on, “Ben wants me to understand my worth and know my value. I feel even more relaxed and comfortable, which makes me feel even more beautiful than I have ever felt with someone else."

    Ben and Jen tied the knot last year after rekindling their romance in 2021, nearly two decades after calling off their engagement, which ended in the early 2000s following high media attention around the couple.

    This isn’t the first time Jen has gushed about her new husband this year. In May, she opened up about the director as a father. Ben has three children from his past marriage to Jennifer Garner, which ended in 2018, while Lopez is a mom to twins that she shares with her ex-husband Marc Anthony.

    Lopez Is Reportedly Worried About Divorce

    While Lopez’s sweet words seem to contradict with reports of relationship problems between the pair, that hasn’t quelled speculation the couple are on the rocks, despite just celebrating their first wedding anniversary. One report from earlier this year claims Lopez asked for a post-nuptial agreement shortly after they got marriage, as she fears divorce is looming.

    "Both of them are realists who have been through divorce and don't want to be dealing with a nightmare money battle if they don't make it out of this rough patch," one source claimed, though they said Lopez is “still giving this marriage everything she’s got.”

    They didn’t confirm if a post-nup was in the works for sure, but said Ben was willing to do what it takes to make Lopez “happy.” Lopez has an estimated net worth of $400 million, while Affleck’s fortune is approximately $200 million.

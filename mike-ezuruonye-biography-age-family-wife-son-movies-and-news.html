    Mike Ezuruonye Biography

    Mike Ezuruonye is a Nigerian actor. His career in Nollywood started accidentally when the famous producer Ruke Amata found him at the filling station and advised him to attend casting calls for the movies.

    He attended the Federal Government College, Taraba, and Archbishop Aggey Memorial school, Lagos before studying Accounting at Nnamdi Azikiwe University.

    He started his acting career in the year 2003. His professional acting debut was a Zeb Ejiro Tv series, titled ‘Ultimate Hero.’ The actor came to the limelight after featuring in the movies ‘Broken marriage’, ‘Critical Decision,’ and ‘Burning Desire.’

    Mike Ezuruonye Age

    Mike was born on September 20, 1982, in Lagos, Nigeria. He is 37 years old as of 2019.

    Mike Ezuruonye Family

    Parents

    He is the eldest in a family of five that was wealthy. His father passed on while he was young and his uncles usurped all Mike, his mother and siblings could lay claims to as inheritance. His mother worked hard to provide for his children and trained Mike and his siblings and today they are all high flyers in their callings.

    His mother is his role model. “My mother, my superwoman. I have never known anyone more positive and hardworking as my mom,” the actor once said in an interview with Best of Nollywood while describing his role model.

    He worked as a banker prior to becoming an actor. In an interview, he said: “The one after me is a successful banker, the next is an electronics engineer with Shell, and the fourth is a medical doctor in the UK, while the fifth is with Chevron. God has guided us real good.”

    Mike Ezuruonye Wife

    He is married to Nkechi Nnorom and the couple has a son name Reynold Nkembuchim Ezuruonye. He married his wife Nkechi on Saturday 13, November 2010 after a traditional marriage ceremony in May 2010.

    Mike Ezuruonye Daughter

    Mike shares a lot of photos with his son Reynold on social media, He’s yet to share that photo of his daughter. This seems like a cautious move to shield his family and private life fro public scrutiny. Despite some malicious rumors and gossip of divorce buzzing around the media, the couple is still together and live in Ontario, Canada.

    Mike Ezuruonye Son

    The couple (Mike and Nkechi Nnorom) have a handsome son, Reynold Nkembuchim, together. The son was born on 1st October 2011 in Canada. The kid is fondly referred to as an “independence baby” since he was born on the Independence day of Nigeria. Reynold has a young sister who is not known to the public as his parents haven’t revealed her face to the limelight.

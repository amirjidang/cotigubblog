    Who Is Meredith Marks Sister? Meet Myra Ruttenberg

    Myra Ruttenberg is the sister of Meredith Marks. Both of the sister sisters have a close relationship and frequently work together to aid others.

    Myra has always had a keen insight into how others think and feel. Myra achieved success as a business owner and event coordinator after earning her degree from the School of Cinematic Arts at the University of Southern California.

    She used her people skills in her profession to negotiate her clients’ emotional demands while emphasizing attention to detail. Meredith and Myra were affected by their parent’s divorce as children, and Myra later helped her children through this adjustment.

    She is aware of how difficult this shift may be for all parties, not just grownups. She discovered how alone it could feel after experiencing the aftereffects of addiction and mental health issues with her son. She saw her son’s use of drugs as self-medication during this period as a cry for assistance.

    She frequently received incorrect information and was misled as she searched for a suitable facility. Myra Ruttenberg, Marks’ sister, founded the ADR Foundation nonprofit to help other families deal with the emotionally taxing and occasionally expensive healing process.

    Myra wants to ensure those in need don’t get stuck without the services they need by establishing the ADR Foundation for Mental Health & Addiction to supply them. She is aware of the importance of these programs, given the dangerous nature of addiction and mental health issues.

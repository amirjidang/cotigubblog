    Biran Histand Mother Tracey Histand

    Brian Histand’s mother Tracey Histand was devasted when she found the news of the death of her missing son.

    Brian disappeared on May 15, 2013, and was last seen walking south on 35th Avenue towards South Mountain, Phoenix, Arizona. His parents, mother Tracey and father Michael, both tried their best to find their son. They put out his missing flyers all around but could not find their son.

    A Facebook page was created to find Brian. Brian’s remains were found in 2015.

    Robert Hoagland Misssing Update

    On July 28, 2013, Robert Hoagland was last seen buying a map along with fuel for his car. This was the last time anyone ever saw Robert before he disappeared.

    Police investigated several sightings of Robert nearby over the next year but failed to find him. His case was featured on an episode of the Investigation Discovery channel series called Disappeared.

    Robert was a local chef and property appraiser. He went missing a day before his wife was returning from an overseas trip. They had three sons and lived on Glen Road in Newtown, Connecticut.

    Are The Two Missing Cases Connected?

    Brian Histand and Robert Hoagland both went missing within three months span. Brian disappeared on May 15, 2013, whereas Robert went missing on July 28, 2013.

    Unfortunately, there has not been any evidence suggesting the two missing cases are connected.

    We could not find any co-relation between the two cases and the timing could just be a coincidence.

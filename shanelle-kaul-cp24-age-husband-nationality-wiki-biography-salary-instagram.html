    TV reporter Shanelle Kaul is a presenter and journalist for CP24.Since she joined the network in August 2018, she has hosted Autoshop and has covered some of the city’s iconic stories. Her radio career began in 2011 with Shaw, who previously worked for the Toronto Global Office, previously with Global Regina and CTV News Edmonton before moving to CP24.

    She was born in Sri Lanka and raised in Toronto, Canada. She studied journalism at Ryerson University. In 2012, she was awarded the “Floyd Chalmers Business Journalism Excellence Award” at The Ticker Club.

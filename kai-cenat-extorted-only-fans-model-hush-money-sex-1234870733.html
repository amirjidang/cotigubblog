    17 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Kai Cenat, one of the biggest Twitch streamers, is claiming that an OnlyFans content creator named Layla Red is attempting to extort him over alleged hush money for sex.

    Over the weekend, the 22-year-old spent time on his channel explaining the oldest play in the book, leaving many of his viewers uncertain about the “truth.”

    The debacle started when Red posted a photo of a naked and slumbering Cenat in bed with her after an alleged bedroom session. Upon the post, Red claimed that she was exposing the popular personality because he refused to pay her “hush money,” which she claims is an extra $5,000 on top of $5,000 that she claims he already paid her.

    However, Cenat went live on Sunday night (April 14) debunking the OF content creator’s claims and threatening to take her to court due to her breaking a Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) that she supposedly signed.

    He also shared a transcript of his Apple Pay transactions, showing proof that he never paid her five stacks for sex, but only $50 for her Uber ride.

    In response, Red also shared a screen recording of her own which apparently shows a message from Cenat sending over $5k via Apple Pay.

    To take matters even more seriously, the New York native claimed to have now purchased her name domain so that any time someone Google’s her, they’ll see “all of her lies,” instead of her lucrative OF link.

    As we wait for a conclusion to the alleged extortion, Kai Cenat has been entertaining his viewers by trolling the woman on his Live. He most recently showed himself “smoking on that Layla Red.”

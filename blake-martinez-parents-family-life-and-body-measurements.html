    Blake Martinez is a professional American footballer who plays as an inside linebacker for the Green Bay Packers of the National Football League (NFL). He started out with the team after he was selected in the fourth round of the 2016 NFL Draft. As a student-athlete, he was one of the best during his days at Canyon del Oro High School and Standford University. In 2015, he was named to the First Team All-Pac-12 team, while in 2017, he claimed the NFL Tackling Co-Leader honors.

    A three sportsman who played basketball, football, and volleyball but later chose football over the trio. After he was born on 9th January 1994, he was named Blake Edmond Martinez. Blake was born in Tucson Arizona and was also raised there alongside his younger siblings.

    For his education, the footballer attended Canyon del Oro High school and played under the supervision of coach Dustin Peace as a tight end, running back, and linebacker. He played with amazing skills for which he earned the PrepStar All-West Region Team honors, and was also named the Defensive Player of the year twice. Upon graduation from high school, Blake was rated as a three-star recruit by Rivals.com and being topnotch in high school, a lot of sports inclined schools offered him scholarship including the University of Arizona, Arizona State University, Northern Arizona University, Stanford University, University of Oregon, and San Diego State University. He later committed to Stanford University.

    All through his days at Stanford, Martinez was active in the school team, he recorded an impressive number of tackles, interceptions, and passes. He never relented in earning praises from his fans and coach as he put his very best in his games. After his collegiate career ended, he declared his intents of joining the National Football League and was later selected by the Green Bay Packers in the fourth round of the 2016 NFL Draft. He inked a four-year contract with the team worth $2.72 million with a signing bonus of $383,393 on May 6, 2016. Ever since then, Blake has shown his relevance to the team in each of their games.

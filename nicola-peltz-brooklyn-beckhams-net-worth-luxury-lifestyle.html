    6 What Is Nicola Peltz And Brooklyn Beckham's Net Worth?

    There are some celebrities who have so much money in the bank that they truly never have to worry about money for a single second. Nicola Peltz and Brooklyn Beckham are definitely in that category. They not only come from rich families, but they have been earning cash on their own for a while now. They can afford their lifestyle because they have tens of millions between them.

    According to Celebrity Net Worth, Nicola Peltz has a $50 million net worth. The publication notes that she took on modeling work when she was growing up and then became an actress, and she has money from working steadily over the years.

    As for her husband Brooklyn Beckham, he has a net worth of $10 million. While that is lower than his wife's, it's definitely enough money to cover anything that he wants to pay for. Combined, the couple has a $60 million net worth, which is amazing since they are both in their 20s.

    5 Nicola Peltz And Brooklyn Beckham Purchased A $10 Million Home

    For many people, buying a house for $1 million would be tough. Maybe they can make some sacrificies and figure it out... but for the most part, that might be all but a dream. For celebrities, of course, that is considered a pretty affordable piece of property. It doesn't seem that Nicola Peltz and Brooklyn Beckham have to even think twice about purchasing any home that they want... at least judging by how much they spent on their very first one.

    She said, "Honestly, we've talked about it so much. And I think that we would want to definitely have a house here just because so much of our work is here. It was so silly of us. And now we're saving up money to get our dream house. But we both honestly love New York so much. If our work was there, we'd be there in five seconds."

Later, we'd return to the boat and take a swim in the water, followed by dinner and a few rounds of roulette at the local casino."

    In the summer of 2023, they went on a very nice vacation and boarded a yacht in St. Tropez with Greg and Bradley, Nicola's siblings.

    Austin Swift Biography

    Austin Swift is a well-reputed handsome American actor and also the younger brother to famous pop sensation “Taylor Swift”. he turned actor with his debut performance in Ben Affleck’s crime drama ‘Live By Night’. After he attended theGolden Globe and People’s Choice Awards with his sister, the media went berserk over the handsome Swift.

    Austin Swift Family and Early Life

    He was born on March 11, 1992, in Wyomissing, Pennsylvania, United States; two years after the birth of Taylor Swift. His father, Scott Kingsley Swift, worked as a stockbroker and his mother, Andrea Swift, is a homemaker. In 2015, his mother was diagnosed with cancer and received treatment for the same.

    The news was devastating for Austin and Taylor Swift, as both have always idolized their mother. However, with the encouragement from his family, he launched his Hollywood career and began starring in movies in 2016. He is presently working for the film ‘Cover Versions’, which is yet to hit the box office.

    Austin Swift Career

    During his college years, he worked as a freelancing photographer for ‘Getty Images’ and also snapped his sister for the ‘People Magazine’. He also performed in several plays such as ‘Six Characters in Search of An Author’ and ‘Dead Man’s Cell Phone’. Theatre redefined his career choice and exposed him to the world of entertainment.

    Thus, in 2016 he was cast in a cameo role in Ben Affleck’s ‘Life By Night’ as Mayweather. The same year he worked in the movie ‘I.T’ alongside Pierce Brosnan as ‘Lance’. In 2017, he will be seen playing the lead role of a band member, in the mystery thriller ‘Cover Versions’.

    Austin is not only strikingly charming but also hardworking. After graduating from university, he has worked in three films within two years, and the third being a lead role. His unconditional love for photography has driven his ‘Instagram’ account to extreme heights. It is quite evident, that he can capture a zillion thoughts and emotions in just a frame.

    Thus, his talent in both acting and photography is equally placed, and he seems to be giving both his passions equal priorities. It is unimaginable, that he managed to stay away from all the limelight and dedicate his time towards his education with his sister being rated as the number one country artist of her era. But Austin stayed humble and worked his way to fame on his own strength.

    Why the 'Nine Perfect Strangers Wig' Stands Out

    Yes, Kidman did in fact, rock a wig. The long pale blonde wig featured in Nine Perfect Strangers is set apart because it is actually good. The length of the wig aids Kidman to bring the character of Masha Dmitrichenko to life. During the filming of the Hulu production, Kidman leaned into method acting. She would only respond to her character's name to allow the character to be authentic.

    The story focuses on nine individuals that signed up for a 10-day self-development program. They all agree to give themselves wholly to Masha's care in complete isolation. Little do they know, Masha has nefarious methods. The character of Masha is based on Australian author Liane Moriarty's novel also titled Nine Perfect Strangers. The TV adaptation premiered in August 2022.

    The John Wick Garage: A Peek Inside Keanu Reeves Insane Car Collection

    Keanu Reeves, the beloved actor who captivated audiences with his performances in iconic films such as The Matrix and John Wick, is not only celebrated for his on-screen talent, but also for his extraordinary passion for automobiles.

    27 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Highlights

    • Keanu Reeves' car collection is insane, featuring a rare Volvo 122, a Mercedes-Benz GLE SUV, and a vintage Porsche 911 Carrera.
    • Reeves is lucky enough to own not one, but three Ferraris, including the California T, 488 GTB, and LaFerrari.
    • The ultimate speed champion in Reeves' garage is the Bugatti Veyron, known for its impressive 8.0-liter W16 engine and relentless power.

    Keanu Reeves, the beloved actor who captivated audiences with his performances in iconic films such as The Matrix and John Wick, is not only celebrated for his on-screen talent, but also for his extraordinary passion for automobiles.

    From luxury vehicles to cutting-edge supercars, Reeves' car collection is nothing short of insane.

    In this exclusive journey, let’s take a peek inside the insane garage of Keanu Reeves, where luxury, speed, comfort, and sophistication collide in a symphony of exceptional vehicles.

    Let’s venture into the world of this Hollywood superstar's automotive obsession and explore the stunning array of vehicles that make up his giant car kingdom.

    8 Volvo 122

    $24,500

    Keanu Reeves, an enthusiast of enduring classics, proudly owns a rare gem in his collection: the Volvo 122. This vehicle stands as one of the Swedish automaker's most exquisite and timeless models, boasting a rich heritage within the world of automobiles.

    Resplendent in Vintage Green, Keanu's 122 exudes an impeccable allure, effortlessly capturing attention on the streets, even in the presence of his supercars. Although the 122's specifications do not position it as a speedster, this Swede possesses a charm beyond velocity.

    Nestled beneath its hood lies a modest 1.8-liter four-cylinder engine, delivering just shy of a hundred horsepower. This power is ample for transporting Keanu from one destination to another, all the while exuding an air of regal sophistication.

    7 Mercedes-Benz GLE

    $74,000

    A premium SUV is an essential component of any well-rounded garage, and Keanu Reeves' collection is no different. To fulfill this requirement, he opted for a Mercedes-Benz GLE, a standout choice in the SUV category.

    This vehicle seamlessly aligns with the actor's style and has been frequently observed on the road, transporting him in lavish comfort and opulence. The GLE stands as a direct competitor to the BMW X5, offering a perfect blend of performance and aesthetics.

    Reeves' GLE is equipped with a robust 4.7-liter V8 bi-turbo engine, delivering ample power to ensure effortless highway journeys. Imagining John Wick behind the wheel of this car, perhaps with a few stylistic enhancements, could undoubtedly elevate an already impressive image.

    6 Porsche 911 Carrera

    $117,100

    Among Keanu Reeves' impressive car collection, a vintage 993 Porsche 911 Carrera stands out as one of the most exceptional vehicles. Regarded as a classic due to its reputation as an accessible yet high-performance sports car in its era, this 911 model ranks among the finest ever produced by Porsche throughout its storied history.

    Reeves' Carrera is adorned with a sleek dark gray wrap, further enhancing its allure as a timeless classic. While the 911 Carrera in Reeves' insane car collection might not boast the performance figures of contemporary sports cars, it still packs plenty of punch for spirited driving.

    5 Ferrari California T

    $300,000

    Every car enthusiast aspires to own a Ferrari at some juncture in their existence. Keanu Reeves, on the other hand, is fortunate enough to have not just one but three of these iconic vehicles.

    Among this trio, the first is the Ferrari California T, the convertible masterpiece from the Prancing Horse stable, which effortlessly transforms open-air driving into the epitome of coolness. Ferrari's California T embodies the essence of a premium grand tourer, sports car, and convertible all rolled into one exceptional package.

    Keanu Reeves has been sighted enjoying the sheer thrill of driving it, evident in the joy radiating from his expressions. It's indeed gratifying to witness The Matrix's star appreciating what is arguably one of the most underrated Ferraris in history.

    4 Bentley Continental GT

    $320,000

    Keanu Reeves is both fortunate and filled with pride as the owner of a Bentley Continental GT, a vehicle that ranks among the most powerful and sought after Bentley models globally. This car seamlessly caters to the actor's desires for both opulence and top-tier performance.

    While the Continental GT might be considered a luxury beyond the reach of most, but thanks to his highly successful acting career, Reeves can comfortably afford such a prized possession. His decision is certainly well-founded, as his wealth secured him a formidable machine equipped with a 4.0-liter twin-turbo V8 engine, recognized as one of the premier powerplants in its class.

    This engine undoubtedly empowers the Bentley to achieve speeds that wouldn't seem out of place in one of John Wick's cinematic car chases, with the hero emerging from the vehicle as fresh as a daisy.

    3 Ferrari 488 GTB

    $320,000

    Keanu Reeves' collection includes a second "Prancing Horse," the remarkable Ferrari 488 GTB. With a keen eye for legendary automobiles, the actor made an investment in the successor to the legendary Ferrari 458 Italia.

    The 488, in its evolution, swapped out its predecessor's exuberant naturally aspirated engine for the thrill of turbocharged power, resulting in a surge of additional speed that elevated its overall performance to new heights.

    Reeves undoubtedly appreciates the 3.9-liter twin-turbo V8 of the 488 GTB, which exhibits minimal turbo lag and effortlessly propels the car to impressive speeds. The vehicle's on-road performance is razor-sharp, providing the driver with an exceptional level of feedback that surpasses what most of its competitors can deliver.

    2 Bugatti Veyron

    $1.2 Million

    The Ferrari LaFerrari offers an abundance of speed. However, Keanu Reeves seeks performance beyond what most consider the pinnacle.

    In his garage, the ultimate speed champion is the Bugatti Veyron, a machine designed with the sole mission of attaining maximum velocity.

    The Veyron is engineered solely for velocity, and the exhilarating manner in which it accomplishes this feat is a life-affirming experience, as connoisseurs like Reeves are well aware. Its exceptional quality lies in the presence of an impressive 8.0-liter W16 engine, quadruple-turbocharged to deliver relentless power at any speed.

    1 Ferrari LaFerrari

    $1.5 Million

    Keanu Reeves rounds off his trio of Ferraris with a member of the revered "holy trinity" of hypercars, the Ferrari LaFerrari.

    The only aspect the actor might question about this incredible car is its peculiar name, considered one of the most unconventional automotive marketing decisions in recent memory. However, when it comes to performance and everything else, the LaFerrari takes it to the highest level.

    Ferrari's LaFerrari is a hybrid hypercar that vividly demonstrates the efficacy of the "full-throttle-everything" philosophy when executed with precision. The fusion of its powerful 6.3-liter V12 engine and a solitary electric motor is undeniably remarkable.

    It could easily be considered the standout gem in Keanu Reeves' car collection, a vehicle he will likely cherish for years to come.

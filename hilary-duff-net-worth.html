    Hilary Erhard Duff, born in Houston, Texas, USA, Hilary Duff is an actress, a singer and a moderator of TV shows, with an estimated net worth of $25 million.

    Hilary has proven that she is the only artist that truly rivaled with pop singer Britney Spears. She was first introduced to the public by the TV program "Lizzie McGuire" on the Disney Channel. She entered in the film industry by playing in films such as "Western True Women" (1997), "Casper" (1998), playing the role of Wendy in "Soul Collector" (1999).

    In 2002, Duff has expressed the desire to sing and took the decision to pursue a career in music. She debuted with the song "Santa Claus Lane" that was selected to be the soundtrack for the movie "The Santa Clause 2". She starred in "Agent Cody Banks" and then went to Italy where she discovered that everyone knew her thanks to her music. The songs that have been most successful have been "Why Not" and "I Can not Wait".

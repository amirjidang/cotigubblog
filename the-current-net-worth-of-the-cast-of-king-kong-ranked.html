    King Kong is a monster action film released in 2005, produced, written, and directed by Peter Jackson. The film follows the story of a group of filmmakers exploring an uninhabited mystery island where they face a giant gorilla, and it is the remake of the 1933 film of the same name. The film stars major Hollywood A-listers like Naomi Watts, Jack Black, Adrien Broody, and Andy Serkis in significant roles. The movie won three Best Visual Effects, Best Sound Editing, and Best Sound Mixing awards at the 2006 Academy Awards.

    King Kong is still known for its groundbreaking CGI and action sequences. It was a major commercial success grossing around $556.9 Million worldwide. Below is a list of all the primary cast members of King Kong ranked by their net worth.

    Thomas Kretschmann - $8 Million

    Thomas Kretschmann is a German actor known for supporting roles in movies like Resident Evil: Apocalypse, The Pianist, U-571, In Enemy Hands. He has also appeared as Wolfgang von Strucker in MCU biggies like Captain America: The Winter Soldier and Avengers: Age of Ultron. Thomas plays the role of Captain Englehorn in King Kong. He is also quite active in European cinema and TV shows, winning various awards like the Capri Legend Award, the Puchon International Fantastic Film Festival award for Best Actor, etc. He has an estimated net worth of $8 Million as per Celebrity Net Worth.

    Adrien Brody - $10 Million

    A critically acclaimed actor, Adrian Brody shot to fame after his role in the 2002 Roman Polinski-directed war drama movie The Pianist. He played the iconic role of Władysław Szpilman in the film for which he won the Academy Awards for Best Actor In A Leading Role, becoming the youngest actor to win this award. Apart from an Academy award, he has also won a César Award and has been nominated for multiple prestigious awards. As per IMDB, he played the role of writer Jack Driscoll in King Kong and drew a $10 Million paycheck for his role. He has also starred in movies like Midnight in Paris, The Grand Budapest Hotel and TV shows like Peaky Blinders and Houdini. Brody has an estimated net worth of $10 Million currently.

    Jamie Bell - $11 Million

    Jamie Bell is an English actor and dancer known for starring in popular movies like The Adventures of Tintin, Fantastic Four, Jumper, Film Stars Don't Die in Liverpool, etc. He is often tagged as a versatile actor, and he has won a BAFTA award for Best Actor for his debut role in the 2000’s Billy Elliot. Apart from movies, he has starred in TV shows like TURN: Washington's Spies and Shining Girls. Jamie plays the role of Jimmy in King Kong and has an estimated net worth of $11 Million, as reported by The Richest.

    Kyle Chandler - $12 Million

    Kyle Chandler is an Emmy-winning actor mostly known for his iconic role as Eric Taylor in the hit TV show Friday Night Lights. He is popular for his roles in famous movies like The Wolf Of Wall Street, Argo, Zero Dark Thirty, and the 2016 classic Manchester By The Sea. Besides film, he has appeared in TV shows like Grey's Anatomy, The Lyon's Den, Bloodline, etc. He plays the narcissistic film star Bruce Baxter in King Kong. Owing to his regular appearances in movies and TV shows, Chandler has accumulated a net worth of $12 Million.

    Colin Hanks - $14 Million

    The son of the legendary actor Tom Hanks, Colin Hanks is an actor, director, and producer. He made his acting debut in 1999 with the sci-fi series Roswell. Colin is mainly known for his roles in TV shows like The Good Guys, Dexter, Fargo, etc., and has also appeared in movies like Orange County, Untraceable, Jumanji: The Next Level, etc. He might not be as successful as his father, but he has earned a net worth of $14 Million over his 20 years career in Hollywood. Colin plays the role of Preston in King Kong.

    Andy Serkis - $18 Million

    Andy Serkis is the world's most popular CGI motion capture actor. He is the face behind various iconic CGI characters like Gollum from the Lord Of The Rings franchise, Caesar in the Planet Of The Apes trilogy, Supreme Leader Snoke in the Star Wars sequel trilogy, and the list goes on. Apart from being a motion capture actor Serkis is a compelling performer while playing conventional roles. He has won and been nominated for various prestigious awards, including BAFTA and Emmy. Serkis plays the motion capture role of the mighty Kong in the movie King Kong. He is already an industry veteran with a net worth of $18 million.

    Naomi Watts - $35 Million

    Naomi Watts is an Oscar-nominated British Actress known for her roles in Hollywood movies and TV shows. She started acting in 1986 and has been a part of several critically and commercially successful movies like Mulholland Drive, The Ring, 21 Grams, Birdman (The Unexpected Virtue of Ignorance), The Divergent Series, etc. Naomi is one of the most successful actresses in Hollywood, and she plays the role of Ann Darrow, an aspiring actress in the 2005’s King Kong. She won the Saturn Award for Best Actress for her performance as the estranged damsel in distress. As reported by The Things, she reportedly drew a paycheck of $5 million for her role in King Kong and has accumulated a net worth of $35 million till now.

    Jack Black - $50 Million

    The richest of all the cast of King Kong, Jack Black, is an actor, comedian, musician, writer, and producer. The multi-talented actor is also the founding member of the award-winning band Tenacious D. Jack Black has established himself as one of the go-to comic actors of Hollywood with roles in various movies like Demolition Man, High Fidelity, Shallow Hal, Envy, Margot at the Wedding, Gulliver's Travels, Jumanji film series, etc. He has also lent his voice to the character of the lazy panda, Po, in the hugely popular Kung Fu Panda film series. He has been honored with a star on the Hollywood Walk Of fame for his outstanding contribution to cinema. Jack plays the stubborn and notorious film director Carl Denham in King Kong. With his multi-talented self, Jack Black has accumulated a whopping net worth of $50 million.

    The movie King Kong boasts a very reputed cast of some of the most prominent stalwarts of Hollywood. While there is no comparison regarding the talent of all the cast members, Jack Black and Naomi Watts stand out as the wealthiest in the lost. Jack is the richest cast member, with a net worth of $50 Million, followed by Watts with $35 Million.

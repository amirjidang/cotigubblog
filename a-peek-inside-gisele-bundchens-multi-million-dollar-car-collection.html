    Being on Forbes’ 2011 100 Most Powerful Women list and the 3rd Youngest Power Woman 2011, it's no wonder that Gisele Bundchen's car collection would make her garage a luxurious one. This powerful woman is a Brazilian fashion model aged 42 years old. She was born Gisele Caroline Bundchen and was 2017’s 16th richest woman in the entertainment industry according to Forbes ranking. Being one of the highest-paid models in the world with an estimated net worth of $400 million, it is quite obvious why we need to take a peek into her luxurious garage. With such fortune, what kind of cars does Gisele grace her garage with? Continue reading to find out!

    Jeep Grand Cherokee ($44,000)

    Via Wikimedia Commons

    This car appears to be every family’s dream car because it is an ideal mix of luxury, style, and comfort. Giselle acquired its 2012 model at the rate of $44,000 and it is notably one of the most splendid SUVs in the car market which is why the richest and celebrities alike rush to purchase it without hesitation. Despite not being a flashy or pricey ride, the Jeep Grand Cherokee is definitely a safe and reliable car and this was in fact the car Giselle was seen most with while she was expecting her second child.

    Lexus RX400H ($47,000)

    Via Wikimedia Commons

    This is one amazing car in Gisele’s car collections. She acquired the Lexus RX400H in 2009 at the rate of $47000 which might have been due to the birth of her son in the following year as the car’s tenacity and electronic features makes it a perfect family car to run personal errands. She seems to be quite conscious of her environment and has a knack for low-emission or hybrid cars.

    BMW X5 ($59,400)

    Via Wikimedia Commons

    This BMW X5 SUV was first released in the year 1999, it is a medium-sized fourth-generation luxury SUV that has had about 4 well-defined iterations so far. It is the perfect car for a family of four and one can understand why this would be a worthy purchase for Gisele at the worth of about $59,400. It is powered by a 3.0-liter turbocharged Inline-6 engine which generates about 335 horsepower, it is equipped with BMW’s first-class features, so, luxury definitely still has a stronghold here. The BMW X5 can go from 0 to 60 mph in less than 6 seconds and has a top speed of 130 mph.

    Audi A8 ($86,000)

    Via Wikimedia Commons

    The Audi A8 is one of the distinctive cars in Gisele Bundchen’s luxury car collection despite its price. The Audi A8 which costs $86,000 has a 4.2-liter V8 engine that generates 335 HP with 317 Ib-ft of torque. In the early 2010s, the Audi A8 was amongst the big shots in fashion due to its sophisticated exterior which encompasses its breathtaking proficiency in terms of its comfort and performance

    Audi S8 ($118,995)

    Via Wikimedia Commons

    Gisele seems to be a lover of the Audi brand as she has yet another Audi model in her luxurious garage boasts of a 3,993 V8 TFSI engine that powers 510HP and provides ambiance even during city traffic. One of its best features includes an acceleration from 0 to 60 mph in less than 4 seconds and could complete a quarter mile at 118 mph in less than 12 seconds.

    Range Rover HSE LUX ($190,000)

    Via Wikimedia Commons

    As one of Gisele’s favorites due to her environmental consciousness, this Range Rover is a mix of luxury, comfort, and convenience. Acquired in black color, the Range Rover HSE LUX features a 3.0-liter supercharged V6 in combination with an 8-speed automatic transmission that generates 345 HP. The car was acquired at a fair price of $190,000.

    Mercedes-Benz SL-Class ($222,000)

    Via Wikimedia Commons

    Despite being around for about 70 years, the Mercedes Benz Sports Lightweight is a grand tourer and it is one of the best cars in Gisele’s luxury car collection. Its detachable hardtop convertible features an elaborate road presence, it was purchased at a high price of $222,000 and it features a perfect combination of comfort, sports, and luxury.

    Rolls Royce Ghost ($400,000)

    Via Wikimedia Commons

    For the rich, Rolls Royce is a symbol of prestige for the Uber strata of today’s society and this is not just due to its massive price but also due to its elegance. Due to its exorbitant price, Rolls Royce is made for only the wealthiest who can effortlessly afford this legendary luxurious car and Gisele Bundchen is on the list of elites who own a Rolls Royce Ghost in the color Diamond Black. The Rolls Royce Ghost was purchased at the price of $400,000 and its sleek features include a 6.75-liter V12 engine that powers over 562 HP with 780 Nm of torque which is more than enough to tow the heavy weight of the car fashionably.

    Rolls Royce Cullinan ($600,000)

    Via Wikimedia Commons

    Only the mega-rich elites own the Rolls Royce Cullinan with a weight of 6,000 Ibs and just like the Rolls Royce Ghost which already graces Gisele’s luxurious garage, the Rolls Royce Cullinan is powered by a 6.7-liter Twin Turbocharged V12 engine which is generated by a 563 HP. The Rolls Royce Cullinan is also referred to as the British SUV and it comes at the handsomely high price of $600,000

    Guide for Stardew Valley - Farming

    28 Aug 2024, 00:00

    This section will cover the Farm levels, fertilizers, animal produce, various crops, their value, and how long they take to harvest. For reference, the price the items sell for is normal, silver, gold, and if applicable, iridium (N/S/G/I), which is indicated by the star (or lack of) by the crops icon.

    All crops MUST be watered once a day, or they will die, making your work and money, go poof. You can use fertilizer to make plants grow faster, be worth more, require less watering, and even form Giant versions of themselves.

    Giants sometimes form when a 3x3 grid of the same plant mature, odds can be increased with a 3x4 grid. Giants produce between 15 to 20 of the crop instead of 9, and can only be harvested with an axe. Only Cauliflower, Melons, and Pumpkins can be Giants.

    Skills

    Leveling your Farm skill also provides bonuses to value, speed, and unlocks new crafting options.

    • Level 1 - Can craft Basic Fertilizer and Scarecrows
    • Level 2 - Can craft Mayo Machine, Stone Fence, and Sprinkler
    • Level 3 - Can craft Bee-hive, Speed Gro, and Farmers Lunch
    • Level 4 - Can craft reservative Jar, Basic Retaining Soil, and Iron Fence
    • Level 5 - Player can pick either the "Rancher" perk (animal produce worth 20% more), or "Tiller" perk (crops worth 10% more) perks
    • Level 6 - Can craft Cheese Press, Quality Sprinkler, and Hardwood Fence
    • Level 7 - Can craft Loom, and Quality Retaining Soil
    • Level 8 - Can craft Oil Maker, Keg, and Deluxe Speed Gro
    • Level 9 - Can craft Seed Maker, Iridium Sprinkler, and Quality Fertilizer
    • Level 10 - Player can pick either "Coop Master" perk (befriend Coop animals quicker and half incubation time), "Shepard" perk (befriend Stable animals quicker and sheep produce wool quicker), "Artisan" perk (artisan goods worth 40% more), or "Agriculturist" perk (crops grow 10% quicker)

    Fertilizer

    • Basic and Quality Fertilizer (Lvl 1 & 9) - Raises chance to produce quality crops
    • Basic and Quality Retaining Soil (Lvl 4 & 7) - Soil may stay watered overnight
    • Basic and Deluxe Speed Gro (Lvl 3 & 8) - Reduces grow time by 10/25%, does not impact additional harvests
    • Trellis plants CANNOT be walked through, trellis plants are indicated by name.
    • Fertilizer, Giants, and Unlocked Skills are NOT taken in to consideration in the numbers below.
    • "Cost" assumes you buy from the General Store, unless noted.

    Aruban professional baseball player Xander Bogaerts has an estimated net worth of $980 thousand.

    Xander Bogaerts started his career when he was discovered by Mike Lord, Boston Red Sox's scout in 2009. He made his professional debut in 2010 for the Dominican Summer League Red Sox.

    In 2012, he played for the Portland Sea Dogs, and participated in the All-Star Futures Game. Bogaerts ranked 6th in the MLB.com's Top Prospects list. After a year, he was promoted to Pawtucket Red Sox and finally landed to the Majors.

    Xander Jan Bogaerts was born on October 1, 1992 in San Nicolaas, Aruba.

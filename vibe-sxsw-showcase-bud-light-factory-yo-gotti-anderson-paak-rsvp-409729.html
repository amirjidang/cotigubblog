    Austin’s South by Southwest music festival is next week, and VIBE is set to represent at the annual fest in a big way. Join us for a stacked lineup with some of the hottest artists at the festival at VIBE‘s afternoon showcase on Thursday, March 17 at the Bud Light Factory at Brazos Hall from 12 p.m. to 5 p.m.

    Fresh off the release of his long-awaited The Art of Hustle, the event will be headlined by Memphis rapper Yo Gotti, along with sets from breakout singer/rapper/producer Anderson .Paak and the Free Nationals, rising R&B star BJ The Chicago Kid, New York chanteuse Justine Skye, up-and-coming Bay Area rapper Nef The Pharaoh, multi-talented London rapper/musician/actress Little Simz, and Mike WiLL Made-It protégé Eearz. Trackstar the DJ — the man behind the decks for Run the Jewels and Killer Mike — will be spinning between sets throughout the day.

    Find the full schedule below, and RSVP here. SXSW badge holders welcome. The event is strictly 21+.

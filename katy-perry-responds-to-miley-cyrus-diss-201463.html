    First comes kissing, then comes spanking? Katy Perry diplomatically and cheekily responded to Miley Cyrus' big diss on Thursday, Mar. 6, shortly after the Bangerz singer made some questionable remarks about her pal's dating history.

    "Oooo gurrrl I'm gonna give you the BIGGEST spanking when I see you in the UK bb!" Perry responded directly to Cyrus, along with emojis of a tongue and British flag.

    The "Roar" singer's funny post was in response to several tweets shared by Cyrus Thursday afternoon, slamming Perry's appearance on Australian talk show Sunrise earlier that week: "I just walked up to her to give her like a friendly girly kiss, you know, as girls do," Perry said Tuesday, Mar. 4.

    "And then she like tried to move her head and go deeper and I pulled away," the singer revealed on how the kiss went down. "God knows where that tongue has been. We don't know! That tongue is so infamous!"

    Kris Jenner is a famous American media personality, socialite, and businesswoman best known as the matriarch of the infamous Kardashian family.

    Kristen Mary Jenner was born on November 5, 1955, in San Diego, California, United States as the eldest of two children born to Mary Jo “M. J.” Shannon Campbell and Robert True “Bob” Houghton.

    Kris Jenner’s mother owned a children’s clothing store, while her father Robert Houghton worked as an engineer. Kris rose to fame starring in the reality television series Keeping Up with the Kardashians. Her parents divorced when she was seven years old.

    Kris Jenner was raised alongside her younger sister, Karen Casey Houghton. She attended Clairemont High School and graduated in 1973. She also worked for American Airlines as a flight attendant for a year in 1976.

    Kris Jenner Mom

    Kris Jenner’s mother is Mary Jo “M. J.” Shannon Campbell. She worked as the owner of a children’s clothing store

    Kris Jenner Siblings

    Kris Jenner has a sister named younger sister, Karen Casey Houghton and a stepbrother, Steven “Steve” Shannon from her mother’s marriage to businessman Harry Shannon.

    Kris Jenner Age

    Kris Jenner is 65 years old as of 2021.

    Kris Jenner Children

    Kris Jenner is the mother of six children. She has four children from her first marriage to lawyer Robert Kardashian: Kourtney, Kim, Khloé and Robert, as well as two other children from her second marriage to television personality and retired Olympic Games medalist Bruce Jenner (now Caitlyn): Kendall and Kylie.

    Kris Jenner Grandchildren

    Kris Jenner has 10 grandchildren. They include Mason Dash Disick (b. December 14, 2009), Penelope Scotland Disick (b. July 8, 2012) and Reign Aston Disick (b. December 14, 2014) whose parents are Kourtney Kardashian and Scott Disick.

    Additionally, there is North West (b. June 15, 2013), Saint West (b. December 5, 2015), Chicago West (b. January 15, 2018) and Psalm West (b. May 10, 2019), whose parents are Kim Kardashian and Kanye West.

    There is also Khloé Kardashian and Tristan Thompson’s daughter True Thompson (b. April 12, 2018) as well as Rob Kardashian’s daughter, Dream Renée Kardashian (b. November 10, 2016) whom he shares with ex Blac Chyna. Last but not least is Stormi Webster (b. February 1, 2018) who belongs to Kylie Jenner and Travis Scott.

    Kris Jenner Net Worth

    Kris Jenner has a net worth estimated to be $190 million.

    Kris Jenner Boyfriend

    Kris Jenner is in a relationship with Corey Gamble, a business executive and talent manager who she has been dating for six years. Corey has been known for regularly appearing on episodes of KUWTK.

    Corey Gamble and Kris have a 25-year age gap but their birthdays are only five days apart. Corey is exactly the same age as Kris’ second eldest daughter, Kim Kardashian. The pair met back in August 2014 at fashion designer Riccardo Tisci’s 40th birthday party in Ibiza which was thrown by Kanye West.

    Corey Gamble is originally from Atlanta, Georgia and was born on 10th November 1980. He had studied business marketing at Morehouse College in Atlanta. He is presently living in LA with Kris Jenner.

    Kris Jenner Husband

    Kris Jenner is presently not married. Her first marriage was to lawyer Robert Kardashian (best known for his early legal representation of O. J. Simpson) on July 8, 1978. They divorced in March 1991 and he later died from oesophagal cancer in 2003.

    Kris Jenner’s second marriage was in April 1991, to retired Olympian Bruce Jenner, who publicly came out as a transgender woman in 2015. They announced their separation in October 2013, with Kris filing for divorce, citing irreconcilable differences on September 22, 2014. The divorce became final on March 23, 2015.

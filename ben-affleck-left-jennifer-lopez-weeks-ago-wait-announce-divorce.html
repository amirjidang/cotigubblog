    Ben Affleck Allegedly Left Jennifer Lopez Weeks Ago As They Wait To Announce Divorce

    Despite presenting a united front at one of their kids performances last week, the rumors continue to circulate about Ben Affleck and Jennifer Lopezs relationship, with one report claiming Ben has been moved out of their house for weeks, as they seemingly prepare themselves to announce their divorce publicly and the wave of backlash

    18 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Highlights

    • Ben Affleck reportedly moved out several weeks ago, fueling rumors of a pending divorce announcement with Jennifer Lopez.
    • Speculation grows as insiders suggest the couple may sell their dream home after growing tired of each other's antics.
    • Concerns about potential financial issues loom as it's revealed that Lopez had insisted on a post-nuptial agreement before their marriage.

    Despite presenting a united front at one of their kids’ performances last week, the rumors continue to circulate about Ben Affleck and Jennifer Lopez’s relationship, with one report claiming Ben has been moved out of their house for weeks, as they seemingly prepare themselves to announce their divorce publicly – and the wave of backlash that will likely ensue.

    Speaking to Us Weekly, a new source said the director moved out “several weeks ago,” reinforcing earlier claims that said the pair are living apart. The couple declined the publication’s request for comment.

    However, the reports remain conflicting, as another source had previously told the publication the pair were facing troubles yet were committed to working through them.

    “Jen and Ben are having issues in their marriage,” the insider shared. “They started having issues a few months ago as Jen had started ramping up work commitments and prepping for her tour.”

    Ben And Jen Are Allegedly Readying A Divorce Announcement

    While the pair remain tight-lipped on their current relationship status, one report suggests a divorce announcement is imminent, and they may simply be waiting for the right time to share the news.

    “The writing is on the wall – it’s over,” a source said to In Touch. “They’re headed for a divorce – and for once, [Ben’s] not to blame!” The insider also alleged Ben has “moved out” of the $60 million mansion he secured with Lopez last year noting he’s growing tired of her antics.

    “He’s focusing on his work and his kids now,” the source went on. “Ben already moved out and they’ll likely have to sell the dream house they spent two years searching for. They’ll never stop loving each other, but she can’t control him, and he can’t change her. There’s no way it could have lasted.”

    They added the reason Ben didn’t join Jen at the Met Gala was because they “decided to call it quits.”

    His absence only fueled speculation about their marital problems, but if the couple is truly over, they may not care about the public’s perception, knowing that a divorce announcement is on its way.

    Ben And Jen Allegedly Need To Sell Their Home

    It’s not confirmed if Lopez and Affleck are putting their mega-mansion on the market, though it’s believable if they are separating. While they own several properties, it’s likely the source was referring to the one they bought last year, after years of search.

    Though the deal was apparently made in all cash, fans became curious after it was revealed that they had taken out a mortgage on the home. Questions about money problems arose, though nothing has been confirmed.

    Even more, last year, it was revealed that Lopez was insisting on a post-nuptial agreement due to her concerns about divorce. Despite both stars having fortunes in the hundreds of millions, they apparently chose not to sign a prenuptial agreement before their marriage, which could complicate matters in the event of a divorce.

    The couple tied the knot in 2022 nearly two decades after calling off their original engagement. But it appears fans will have to wait to see if this marriage stands the test of time or not.

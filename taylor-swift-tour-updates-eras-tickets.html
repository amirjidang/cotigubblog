    21 Aug 2024, 00:00

    THOUSANDS of Taylor Swift fans patiently waited to get their hands on pre-sale tickets as the European leg of her Eras Tour went on sale.

    Taylor Swift will kick off her UK and European shows next year and tickets for her 13 Eras tour dates in Britain were staggered across three days this week to prevent a repeat of January's sale when the website crashed.

    Tickets for her London, Edinburgh and Liverpool shows went live on Monday and Tuesday, with many fans taking to social media to report crashes on the ticket sites.

    Today, the final pre-sale tickets became available for fans, with London and Cardiff shows going on sale.

    Ahead of the sale, Ticketmaster released an urgent warning to fans who were struggling to access the site, as it said on Twitter: "Please clear your cache/cookies and ensure you aren't using any VPN software on your computer/device. If this doesn't help, please try using a different browser/device."

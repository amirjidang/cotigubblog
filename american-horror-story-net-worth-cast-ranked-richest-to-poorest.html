    American Horror Story is a popular anthology series that is loved by fans for its scary storytelling and talented actors. Created by Ryan Murphy and Brad Falchuk, the series has had a successful run with more than ten seasons released and a successful spin-off, American Horror Stories. It has also won 16 Emmy Awards, 2 Golden Globes, and four Critics Choice Awards, and amassing millions of loyal fans across the globe.

    Several actors have appeared on the FX show due to its nature; each season is a different talent with most of the same actors taking on different roles. Everyone from musicians, celebrities, and renowned actors worth millions have been cast on American Horror Story. So who is the richest person to ever be cast? Let’s find out as we rank all the American Horror Story cast's net worths from the richest to the poorest.

    15 Kim Kardashian's Net Worth Is $1.4 Billion

    Business mogul and SKIMS founder Kim Kardashian is one of the richest celebrities to ever join the cast of American Horror Story. The reality star has amassed an impressive fortune as a media personality, brand influencer, entrepreneur and actor.

    Though she has been the subject of scandals throughout her career, Kim has managed to grow a large business empire using her fame to accumulate a net worth of $1.7 billion as of 2023. Kim plays the role of Siobhan Corbyn - a publicist in season 12, American Horror Story: Delicate. Kim's acting in the new season has already been slammed as horrible by fans of the show.

    14 Lady Gaga's Net Worth Is $320 Million

    Joker actress Lady Gaga is a successful pop star who has accumulated millions through her albums, merchandise, and tour sales. She is also an award-winning actress appearing in projects like A Star Is Born and House of Gucci. The singer has appeared in two seasons of the series; American Horror Story: Hotel where she played Countess and American Horror Story: Roanoke.

    However, Gaga left the show after scheduling conflicts and a disagreement with producers on her character's direction in Roanoke. Her departure from the show didn’t stop her career actually it boosted it, and she is now worth $320 million.

    13 Cara Delevingne's Net Worth Is $50 Million

    Model Cara Delevingne is mostly known as a runway model who parties with superstars like Taylor Swift. Before she landed a role in Only Murders In The Building alongside Disney alum Selena Gomez, she walked the runway for fashion houses like Fendi, Dolce & Gabbana, and Burberry.

    The London native got into acting, landing roles in 2015’s Paper Towns, 2016’s Suicide Squad, and Carnival Row alongside Orlando Bloom. The supermodel who plays Nurse Ivy in American Horror Story: Delicate is worth $50 million.

    12 Neil Patrick Harris's Net Worth Is $50 Million

    How I Met Your Motheralum Neil Patrick Harris joined the cast of 2014’s American Horror Story: Freak Show and played Chester Creb. He and his husband, David Burtka were first offered the role of a couple during Murder House, but they turned it down.

    Though he hasn’t returned to the franchise since season four, he is among the richest cast in the show's history. Harris has a net worth of $50 million, which he has made from his work as an actor, voice actor, and author.

    11 Angela Bassett's Net Worth Is $25 Million

    Angela Bassett's work as an actress has propelled her to A-list status and worldwide recognition along with several awards, including two Golden Globes. Bassett has appeared in five different seasons of American Horror Story; Coven, Freak Show, Hotel, Roanoke and Apocolypse.

    Bassett, 65, is most famous for her work in 9-1-1 and Marvel’s Black Panther as King T’Challa’s mother. Black Panther: Wakanda Forever made 1.4 billion at the box office, netting her a large salary that added to her $25 million net worth.

    10 Emma Roberts' Net Worth Is $25 Million

    Another successful actress who has been a part of the FX series is Emma Roberts. She began her career as a child actor starring in popular films like Blow, Nancy Drew, Aquamarine, the Nickelodeon series, Unfabulous and the hit series Scream Queens alongside Ariana Grande. Having appeared in more than 50 projects, including Emma Roberts' six seasons American Horror Story, Roberts has earned a net worth of $25 million.

    9 Zachary Quinto's Net Worth Is $22 Million

    Zachary Quinto has a net worth of $22 million. He is best known for his role as Spock in the Star Trek films. While he has gotten roles in other big projects such as Heroes, Snowden, and Invincible, Quinto is a successful stage actor, with his most popular appearance being in the Broadway's The Glass Menagerie. Quinto has appeared in four seasons of American Horror Story, and each time, he delivers a memorable performance.

    8 Billie Lourd's Net Worth Is $20 Million

    Billie Lourd has been a staple on American Horror Story for years, appearing in six seasons. As daughter to Star Wars actress Carrie Fisher, she inherited a huge chunk of her wealth from her mother, who sadly passed away in 2016. Lourd is also known for her role on Scream Queens. Her net worth is estimated to be $20 million.

    7 Macaulay Culkin's Net Worth Is $18 Million

    Home Alone actor Macaulay Culkin hasn’t done a lot of acting since his hit '90s kid film. The first film grossed $467.6 million at the box office and made the young actor a household name which came with its own challenges.

    “I enjoy acting. I enjoy being on set. I don’t enjoy a lot of the order things that come around it.” he told Esquire in 2020. Culkin played Mickey in American Horror Story: Double Feature.

    6 Sarah Paulson's Net Worth Is $14 Million

    You can’t think of American Horror Story without thinking of Sarah Paulson’s terrifying scream when she’s spooked, afterall she's been in nine seasons so far. She has played over five characters in the anthology series, getting five Primetime Emmy Award nominations. Paulson also has a Golden Globe but for her role in The People vs. O.J. Simpson: American Crime Story.

    Aside from the Ryan Murphy series, Sarah Paulson is widely known for her roles in Cupid and Ratched. The award-winning actress is worth $14 million.

    5 Frances Conroy's Net Worth Is $4 Million

    Frances Conroy also makes the list as a cast member worth $4 million. Many fans might not know but apart from her eight American Horror Story seasons, Conroy has had a lengthy career appearing in '90s shows like Crisis Center and Stark Raving Mad. Recently she has had roles in Six Feet Under, Dead To Me, and The Mist.

    4 Denis O’Hare's Net Worth Is $4 Million

    Tony Award-winning actor Denis O’Hare is known for his roles in Take Me Out, The Good Wife, Sweet Charity, True Blood, and Law & Order: Criminal Intent.

    He appeared in the first season of American Horror Story: Murder House and has since returned to star in five other seasons. Through his career as a singer, actor, and writer, O'Hare has amassed a net worth of $4 million.

    3 Evan Peters's Net Worth Is $4 Million

    Evan Peters’ name is synonymous with American Horror Story. He has portrayed countless villains in the anthology horror series over his nine seasons. In addition, he has also appeared on the big screen in films like X-Men: Days of the Future Past, X-Men: Apocalypse, and X-Men Dark Phoenix.

    Peters' recent role in Netflix’s docu-series as Jeffrey Dahmer garnered mixed fan reactions but won him a Golden Globe for Best Actor in a Limited Series.

    2 Adina Porter's Net Worth Is $2 Million

    Adina Porter’s lengthy career as an actress in over 70 projects has earned her a net worth of $2 million. Her most notable roles were in True Blood, The Newsroom, and The 100.

    Though Adina Porter has been in five seasons of AHS, one of her best portrayals were as Lee Harris in American Horror Story: Roanoke and Dinah Stevens in American Horror Story: Apocalypse. The roles garnered her a well-deserved Primetime Emmy Award nomination for Outstanding Supporting Actress in a Limited Series.

    1 Lily Rabe's Net Worth Is $2 Million

    Lily Rabe has appeared in eight seasons of American Horror Story and recently appeared in Love & Death alongside Elizabeth Olsen. Rabe also had parts in Shrinking and 2022’s The First Lady.

    Aside from acting, she has also done work as a producer on films like Downtown Owl. Despite coming from famous parents as the daughter to actress Gigy Clayburgh and David Rabe, she is worth $2 million.

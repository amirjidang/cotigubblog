    According to a new report, Kris Jenner has no plans to walk down the aisle with her boyfriend, Corey Gamble, in order to protect her impressive net worth of $140 million.

    The Central Recorder says that a source recently claimed the momager has little faith a marriage between them would work out due to their big age difference – Kris is 25-years older than her boyfriend. “If Corey eventually wants kids, Kris is much too old to give him a biological child,” they continued.

    RELATED: The Lawsuits Against Travis Scott Are Now Worth Over $10 Billion

    The same insider added that Kris is aware of their differences in fortune. While Kris has nine figures to her name, Corey is only worth a reported $3 million. She fears he would be entitled to a large sum (or at least try to go after it) should they marry but the relationship come to an end later on.

    “There are millions of reasons not to marry,” the anonymous source finished by saying.

    Throughout the years, there’s been speculation as well as accusations that Corey is with the businesswoman mainly for her social status and wealth.

    Speaking to Daily Mail in 2014, Sheree Buchanan – who dated Corey for three years – accused him of being a social climber and warned Kris he was with her for the benefits. “He’s all about power and money and will do whatever it takes to get it,” she said.

    Sheree also alleged Corey was violent with her during their relationship and became “stalkerish” following the break-up. She added that Kris should “sleep with one eye open,” and if they do get married, they should “get a prenup first.”

    Corey is a tour manager and music marketing executive. He first met Kris in 2014 when they both attended fashion designer Riccardo Tisci’s birthday party. The following year, Corey started appearing on Keeping up with the Kardashians as Kris’ boyfriend, despite her children’s hesitation.

    Last July, a report said that Corey was pressuring Kris to increase his allowance. A source claimed he’s only paid a few thousand dollars per months and it was getting “on his nerves,” Suggest explains. They added the music executive feels upset that Kris is more generous with her children than them.

    This isn’t the first time it’s been reported that Kris has no plans to walk down the aisle again – she’s had two unsuccessful marriages, first to attorney Robert Kardashian and then Olympian Caitlyn Jenner (who was then known as Bruce).

    In September 2020, sources told The Sun the matriarch will “never get married again” nor even engaged, adding their current relationship “works for her.”

    Elizabeth Landers Biography

    Elizabeth Landers is a renowned American Journalist and Writer who was born and brought-up in the United States. Currently, she works at CNN in Washington, D.C as a White House Producer, Video Producer for CNN’s election coverage and Freelance News Assistant for the CNN Wires service.

    Prior to joining the CNN news team, she worked at SCENE Magazine/New York Observer as a Contributing Writer. She also served as the Office of State Representative Carl Sciortino, Massachusetts House of Representative and as a Freelance Writer at Daily Front Row.

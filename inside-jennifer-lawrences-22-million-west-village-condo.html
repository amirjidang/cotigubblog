    You may recognize her from the dystopian action film series The Hunger Games, which spawned four movies in total and grossed a whopping $2.970 billion at the box office, but Jennifer Lawrence has starred in dozens of other movies. Born on August 15, 1990, in Louisville, Kentucky, she actually got into acting as a way of dealing with her social anxiety and hyperactivity during her childhood.

    Those acting roles consisted mainly of local church plays and school musicals, but it paid off when she was spotted on the street in New York City during a family vacation when she was just 14 years old. Her talent was immediately detected and she soon signed with a talent agency and began auditioning for roles in Los Angeles. According to Celebrity Net Worth, Her subsequent success as an actress has earned her $160 million, and with it a few trinkets and properties, one of the newest being a $21.9 million townhouse in NYC's West Village. Let's take a look at what this brand new duplex has to offer.

    Living Pretty In The City

    If you're being cast alongside Hollywood stars like Rose Byrne, The Bourne Legacy's Oscar Isaac, Bradley Cooper, and the Jurassic World protagonist Chris Pratt, then you're able to afford one of the priciest condos in New York City. Located on 17 Jane Street between Greenwich and Eighth Avenue in West Village, the property was designed by Sir. David Chipperfield, the head of the international architectural firm bearing his name, according to City Realty.

    The developer of the project, Edward Minskoff, stated that he intentionally sought after a highly adept architect like Chipperfield for the job since his track record of other New York designs includes a redesigned contemporary art wing of the Metropolitan Museum as well as a new headquarters tower for the luxury watch company, Rolex. Edward Minskoff also occupies the top two floors of the building.

    The capacious pad is spread across 6,378 square feet and spans two floors, giving the Oscar-winning actress plenty of living and playing space for her and her husband's first child, who was born in April 2022, reported by E-News. The six-story building displays a red-brick design measuring 85 feet high, which is modest considering it's home to some of the most expansive and extravagant hotels in New York City. Other outstanding exterior features that may have drawn an A-list actress like Lawrence include Roman-course brickwork, traditional lintels, and bronze casement windows. Hopefully, this townhouse would be a much better investment than her Upper East Side penthouse, which she sold at a loss for $9.9 million against a purchase price of $15.6 million back in 2016.

    The Interior Masterpiece

    Fitted with four bedrooms and two master suite bathrooms, Lawrence and her husband will have plenty of sleeping options if the baby decides to keep them awake at night. There is also an elegant walk-in closet. Designed with an open-plan layout, the modern-style chef's kitchen is hand-painted in black and white contrast with a huge center island. Both the kitchen and the sprawling living room are situated on the first floor. In addition, it also boasts its own terrace which gives Lawrence access to a beautifully landscaped garden at the rear of the home, providing a serene hangout spot for romantic dinners or a light get-together.

    The beige wood-paneled flooring also contrasts perfectly with the dark double glass door frames leading to the outdoor garden space as well as the formal dining room table near the great room. The clean white walls also add to the spacious atmosphere and brighten the entire interior with the natural light from outside, not forgetting to mention the 13-foot ceilings that add to the roominess.

    Upstairs are the separate bedrooms which are just as modern, classy, and spacious as the dining and lounging rooms and is equipped wit its own glass door-entrance to the outdoor garden. Also included upstairs are media, lounge, and game rooms just in case the Don't Look Up actress gets bored of the city and decides to do some R&R indoors.

    Additional Features Of The House

    Another portion of the multi-million-dollar duplex, the cellular level, also boasts a laundry room for Lawrence's luxury apparel, and access to the building's gym. Also featured is private elevator providing them access through a private gate and exclusive doorway on to Jane Street. While she may not be able to haul the entire 9 vehicles in her epic car collection, but she should be able to squeeze in the Tesla Model X and maybe her Cadillac Escalade. And while we're on the topic of cars and garages, did you know that the six-story structure was actually erected on the site of a former two-story garage?

    According to Knews, Lawrence and her husband, Cooke Maroney, can now add the new West Village pad to their other Manhattan escape just a stone's throw away at the Tribeca building at 443 Greenwich, which is famous for the many celebrities it houses such as Hollywood power couple Justin Timberlake and Jessica Biel, Top Gun actress Meg Ryan, and actor, comedian, and entrepreneur Ryan Reynolds to name a few.

    Bullet Club Gets Some New Merch

    The Bullet Club has a brand new t-shirt for sale up on their official NJPW shop at prowrestlingtees.com. As seen above, the new design is a red and white take on the classic Bullet Club logo, with the words “Bullet Club Arising” printed on the front, with the traditional logo printed on the arm.

    For those curious, the Club from left to right is Yujiro Takahashi, Chase Owens, Kenny Omega, Bad Luck Fale, ROH World Champion Adam Cole, Tonga Roa (former WWE star Camacho), Tama Tonga, and underneath, IWGP Jr. Tag Team Champions, the Young Bucks. Hangman Page is not pictured.

    NJPW Destruction in Kobe Preview

    Speaking of the Bullet Club, all nine members of the stable will be in action during this weekend’s big NJPW Destruction in Kobe show, streaming live on New Japan World late Saturday night/early Sunday morning. Here’s the match card for that show:


    Michael Elgin (c) vs. Tetsuya Naito

    Jado, Gedo, Kazuchika Okada & Yoshi-Hashi vs. Toru Yano, Naomichi Marufuji, Daisuke Harada & Atsushi Kotoge

    Kushida, Juice Robinson & Hiroshi Tanahashi vs. Bushi, Sanada & Evil

    The Briscoes, Tomohiro Ishii & Hirooki Goto vs. Tama Tonga, Tanga Roa, Bad Luck Fale & Kenny Omega


    Matt Sydal, Ricochet & Satoshi Kojima (c) vs. Adam Cole & The Young Bucks

    Manabu Nakanishi & Yuji Nagata vs. Bobby Fish & Kyle O’Reilly

    Ryusuke Taguchi, Tomoaki Honma & Togi Makabe vs. Teruaki Kanemitsu, Tiger Mask & Hiroyoshi Tenzan

    Captain New Japan & Yoshitatsu vs. Chase Owens & Yujiro Takahashi

    Hirai Kawato, Henare & David Finlay vs. Baretta, Rocky Romero & Will Ospreay

    Payment and Payouts on CJ Affiliate

    As an affiliate marketer, receiving timely payments is crucial for sustaining your business and maximizing your earning potential. CJ Affiliate understands this, which is why they ensure a seamless payment and payout process. Let’s take a closer look at how payments work on the CJ Affiliate program.

    Payment Timeframe

    At CJ Affiliate, payments are made on a net 30 basis. This means that commissions earned in a calendar month will be paid out on the 20th of the following month. For example, if you earn commissions in January, you can expect to receive your payment on the 20th of February.

    Payment Methods

    When it comes to receiving your earnings, CJ Affiliate offers flexible payment options. You can choose to receive payments via check, direct deposit, or CJ International Payments into a Payoneer account. This allows you to select the method that is most convenient for you and ensures that you can access your funds easily.

    Payment Threshold

    Before you can request a payment, there is a minimum payout threshold that you need to reach. For CJ Affiliate, the minimum payout threshold is set at $50. Once your earnings reach this threshold, you can request a payment and receive your hard-earned commissions. This threshold helps to ensure that the administrative costs associated with processing payments are minimized.

    To summarize, CJ Affiliate provides timely payments, flexible payout options, and a reasonable payout threshold to ensure that you can effortlessly receive your earnings. By offering a variety of payment methods and adhering to a net 30 payment cycle, CJ Affiliate prioritizes the financial well-being of its affiliates.

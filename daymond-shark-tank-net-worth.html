    Daymond John: From Humble Beginnings to Fashion Mogul

    Daymond John, born and raised in Brooklyn, New York, had a humble upbringing in Hollis, Queens. He learned the value of hard work at an early age and even balanced a full-time job while attending school. It was during this time that John’s entrepreneurial spirit started to take shape.

    The 1990s marked the beginning of Daymond John’s remarkable journey as he founded the iconic clothing brand FUBU. Standing for “For Us, By Us,” FUBU reflected John’s mission to create a brand that resonated with the African-American community. Starting the company in his mother’s house, John enlisted the help of his friends as co-founders, making FUBU a true testament to teamwork and collaboration.

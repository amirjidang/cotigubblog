    Gwen Stefani and Blake Shelton have been the subject of divorce accusations for months, but that didn’t stop the singer from sharing a sweet post to mark their three year anniversary. But the lovey-dovey caption didn’t go over well with all fans, as she faced some harsh comments following a low-key diss she seemed to make in the message.

    Both of them made sweet posts to commemorate the special day. Blake posted a heartfelt selfie of the couple, while Gwen expressed her enduring love with a touching caption accompanying a carousel of their wedding photos on Instagram.

    Why Fans Thought Gwen’s Message Dissed Her Ex

    "July 3, 2021, it has always been you," Gwen wrote, adding a love-struck emoji. Blake shared his own affectionate message, "Happy anniversary @gwenstefani! I love you!!!!!!!"

    While many fans congratulated the couple, some were critical of Gwen's choice of words. One user commented, "It shows so much insecurity, her need to somehow put her ex-husband down in little, petty ways. It doesn’t give me a (truly) happy vibe."

    In contrast, another fan defended Gwen, offering a blunt response to the critique: "Ok new comer. Google Rossdale and the nanny. You’re just jealous cause it ain’t you!"

    Gwen Believes Blake Is Her Soulmate

    Gwen's marriage to her ex-husband Gavin Rossdale ended in 2016 amid reports that he had been unfaithful, carrying on an alleged affair with their nanny, Mindy Mann, for several years.

    Additionally, another supporter elaborated on the deeper meaning behind Gwen's caption, explaining, "For people that don’t understand the caption: All she is saying is Blake is her true love. She’s never felt how wonderful it could be until she was with him. Have you guys never been in difficult relationships? Yes, she was married before and has children but still… something was missing!!! And this feels much different. Happy Anniversary ????????????????"

    Stefani's journey to stardom began in the late 1980s when she joined the ska band No Doubt as its lead vocalist. The band's breakthrough came in 1995 with their third album, Tragic Kingdom, featuring hits like "Don't Speak." This success catapulted Stefani into the spotlight, establishing her as a prominent figure in the music industry.

    In 2002, Gwen married Gavin, the front man of the rock band Bush. The couple welcomed their first son, Kingston in 2006, followed by Zuma in 2008, and Apollo in 2014. However, their marriage faced turbulence, and they ultimately divorced in 2016 amid reports of Rossdale's infidelity.

    Stefani's life took a positive turn when she met country music star Blake Shelton in 2014 on the set of The Voice, where both served as coaches. Their bond grew stronger over shared experiences, particularly as both were going through divorces at the time—Shelton from fellow country star Miranda Lambert. Their relationship blossomed, and they got engaged in October 2020.

    The pair finally married in 2021. Though they’ve been hit with rumors of relationship woes in recent times, they’re continuing to put on a united front, as evidence by their lovey-dovey messages online.

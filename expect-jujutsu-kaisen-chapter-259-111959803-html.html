    When To Expect Jujutsu Kaisen Chapter 259 Spoilers &amp; Leaks

    Curious about when the Jujutsu Kaisen Chapter 259 spoilers and leaks are coming out? The fight against Yuji and Sukuna becomes more intense. Here is a guide on when to expect the Jujutsu Kaisen Chapter 259 manga plot leaks.

    21 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Curious about when the Jujutsu Kaisen Chapter 259 spoilers and leaks are coming out? The fight against Yuji and Sukuna becomes more intense. Here is a guide on when to expect the Jujutsu Kaisen Chapter 259 manga plot leaks.

    When to Expect Jujutsu Kaisen Chapter 259 spoilers

    Fans can expect the Jujutsu Kaisen chapter 259 spoilers and leaks to be out by Wednesday, May 8, or Thursday, May 9. Weekly Shonen Jump, the manga that serializes JJK, is going on break next week due to the Golden Week holidays in Japan.

    For those in the United States, the spoilers should be out by Wednesday evening or after midnight (Thursday). For those in Asia, that would be approximately Thursday morning or early afternoon. These are just estimates since the release times for the spoilers vary.

    Jujutsu Kaisen Chapter 258 will be out in the U.S. on Sunday, April 28, at 11 AM ET/10 AM CT/8 AM PT.

    The expected release date for Jujutsu Kaisen Chapter 259 is Sunday, May 12.

    Jujutsu Kaisen Chapter 258 Spoilers Recap

    After getting hit by Yuji’s multiple Black Flashes, Sukuna is unable to get his Reverse Cursed Technique (RCT) output back to normal. Sukuna activates Domain Expansion and his surroundings start getting cut. The narration says Sukuna copied his Domain without needing to decrease the output or range after getting attacked by Black Flash.

    In a flashback, the Jujutsu High team discusses RCT. Choso reveals his body allows him to transform his cursed energy into blood. Choso requests pairing Yuji up with an RCT user during the soul-swapping training. Ui Ui reveals his technique can help with swapping souls, but there are limitations.

    Kusakabe says Yuji’s body remembers a special grade’s jujutsu because Sukuna inhabited his body and used his cursed technique. Kusakabe and Yuji will swap so that Yuji can learn cursed energy manipulation and Simple Domain.

    Yuji uses Simple Domain to counter Sukuna’s Domain. The narration says Sukuna can’t keep the Domain on for long. The Domain will fall after 99 seconds, and Yuji’s Simple Domain breaks. He gets slashed but notices Sukuna’s incomplete Domain. Then, Sukuna proceeds to take out his flames.

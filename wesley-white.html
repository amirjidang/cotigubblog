    Famous TikToker Mercedes Tidwell, “Bookclubwannabe” son, has died from an apparent cardiac arrest.

    Wesley White, the beloved son of famous TikToker Mercedes Tidwell and veteran Cameron White, has tragically died in Walker, Louisiana. Mercedes Tidwell shared the devastating news in a heart-shattering video on her TikTok handle ‘Bookclubwannabe.’ She expressed her profound grief over the loss of her beloved son.

    What happened to Mercedes Tidwell’s son, Wesley White?

    Mercedes Tidwell shared in the emotional video that her son Wesley White had suffered a cardiac arrest and passed away on their way to the hospital. In the heartbreaking post, she expressed her disbelief and overwhelming agony over the loss of her son. She said,

    “I don’t even know what to do with myself. I can’t sit around. I feel helpless. Wesley lost his life this morning and I truly can’t understand why this has happened. All I know is I have to put my baby in the ground.”

    Dear Wesley suffered a cardiac arrest and died as the devastated couple was attempting to get him to the hospital for medical care.

    “I will never understand it but Wesley suffered some sort of cardiac arrest. We were on the way to hospital and things just went terribly downhill. I will love him forever. My life revolved around that silly boy. I don’t know where to go from here. I love you all.”

    A GoFundMe page has been established

    Mercedes Tidwell has set up a GoFundMe page to raise funds for her beloved son’s funeral and burial. The goal has been set at 30,000 dollars. The generous Community has raised over 36,000 dollars to help the family. The GoFundMe page says,

    “I didn’t think I would be asking for money to help bury my son. Words can not describe the absolute hole in our lives. Anything helps. Thank you.”

    The Community mourns Wesley White

    The Community has joined together to mourn a beautiful, young soul that has left the earth well before his time, Wesley White. He was the beloved son of Mercedes Tidwell, known for her book reviews and book club on TikTok under the username ‘Bookclubwannabe’ and US Marine Corps veteran and Production Technician at Rubicon LLC, Cameron White.

    The Community has offered condolences and wrapped the family in the heartwarming support. They have shared the grief of the devastated family, sending them heartfelt messages of love, support, and prayers.

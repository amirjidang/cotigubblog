    Ariel Winter Biography – Ariel Winter Wiki

    Ariel Winter Workman is an American actress and voice actress. She was born on January 28, 1998 in Los Angeles, California, United States.

    She was nominated at the Young Artist Awards 2012 as Best Actress in a Motion Picture for her leading role in the film The Chaperone. Winter and her Modern Family cast mates have won four Screen Actors Guild Awards for Best Ensemble in a Comedy Series.

    In October 2012, Winter’s sister Shanelle Workman filed to become her guardian, alleging that their mother had been physically and emotionally abusive.

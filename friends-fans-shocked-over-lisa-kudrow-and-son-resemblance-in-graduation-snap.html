    Lisa Kudrow has shared a doppelgänger picture with her son Julian after he graduated from University of Southern California (USC).

    The Friends star also shared a picture of Julian earlier this month, revealing an impressive resemblance confirmed in the new snap. Kudrow shares Julian with husband Michel Stern, whom she married in 1995.

    Lisa Kudrow Is The Proudest Mom As She Shares A Snap With Graduated Son Julian

    The actress, best known for playing quirky songstress Phoebe Buffay on the NBC sitcom, took to Instagram to share the news.

    “Happy proud HAPPY. And a little crying. By me not him,” she wrote on May 16.

    The mother and son duo shocked fans over their resemblance.

    “He looks just like you!” one fan commented.

    “Pheebo,” another joked on the name of Kudrow’s character in Friends.

    “Congratulations! He looks just like you,” was another comment.

    Friends fans already “met” Julian when Kudrow’s pregnancy was written into the show in season four. Kudrow’s character Phoebe was made the surrogate for her brother Frank’s triplets, incorporating her happy personal news into the series.

    “Is he who was really cooking when you had your brothers triplets?” one fan asked.

    “So you did keep one of Frank’s kids,” another joked.

    "Aww. Seems like yesterday you were trying to stop Rachel from going to London while pregnant," was another comment.

    The comment refers to the show's fourth season finale where the gang, minus a heavily pregnant Phoebe, go to the UK for Ross's wedding.

    Lisa Kudrow’s Son Was Obsessed With Jennifer Aniston

    On an episode of Conan, the actress also revealed her son used to be just as much obsessed with her Friends co-star Jennifer Aniston as the rest of fans.

    “He got a little confused, I know that he was really obsessed with Jen Aniston,” O’Brien began, prompting Kudrow to reply: “No, no, he’d fly into her lap.”

    “Well, she’s a love bug, and that made sense. And I was always glad for anyone that Julian felt love for and felt from,” Kudrow continued, adding: “But then at home, she’d be on TV, and he’d go: ‘Mommy!’”

    Kudrow, Aniston and the other original four Friends — plus a bunch of special guests — will appear on the long-anticipated reunion of Friends, titled “The One Where They Got Back Together”, dropping on HBO Max this May 27.

    What Really Happened Between Shannon Tweed And Gene Simmons?

    It is surreal to think that Gene Simmons and Shannon Tweed have been together since 1983, that spans four decades. The two finally got hitched in 2011, though despite the marriage, it didn't come without its fairshare of struggles.

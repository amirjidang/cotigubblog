    Liza Koshy has blessed her followers with a cute selection of pictures and videos with personal trainer Jenna Willis, prompting many to infer the two are a couple.

    Koshy is best known for her YouTube channel, which boasts nearly 18 million subscribers. She also starred in Netflix 2020 musical comedyWork It, playing the role of Jasmine Hale.

    Liza Koshy Posts Birthday Tribute To New Girlfriend Jenna Willis

    The actress, who has previously been in a relationship with fellow YouTuber David Dobrik, seems to have moved on with Willis. Koshy celebrated her girlfriend in a belated birthday post on Instagram on June 17.

    “happy belated birthday baby • i can't wait to see you at the end of the aisle one day... i just have no idea which role you'll play,” Koshy wrote in the caption.

    The gallery of pictures and videos includes the two getting cozy on holiday and performing a heartfelt rendition of Your Love by English rock band The Outfield.

    "#3, 6, 8 swipes. Drop the [microphone emoji]. Cause we did baby!” Willis commented.

    The personal trainer’s Instagram account also see Koshy’s popping up in her stories, leaving little doubt whether the two are a couple.

    Liza Koshy Fans Are Loving To See Her Happy With Jenna Willis, But Don’t Assume She’s Gay

    Fans have been very supportive of Koshy ever since she introduced Willis to the world.

    “Its 3am and I'm finding out Liza Koshy has a gf life's good,” one fan wrote.

    “LIZA KOSHY HAS A GF?? i mean good for her,” was another comment.

    This seems to be erasing the possibility that Koshy identifies as bisexual or pansexual, as some in the queer community have pointed out.

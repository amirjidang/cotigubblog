    Who Was Jennifer Syme, Keanu Reeves' Late Ex-Girlfriend?

    For as long as Keanu Reeves has been famous, fans have wanted to know about his personal life. But unlike many other celebrities, Keanu likes to keep a low profile, so not much is generally known about his relationships.

    11 Aug 2024, 00:00

    For as long as Keanu Reeves has been famous, fans have wanted to know about his personal life. But unlike many other celebrities, Keanu likes to keep a low profile, so not much is generally known about his relationships.

    However, fans have learned a lot about Keanu's tragic past, including the loss of his daughter and ex-girlfriend.

    And sure, Jennifer Syme dated Keanu Reeves while she was alive, but who was she, and what did she do when not being photographed out with Reeves?

    Jennifer Syme Worked In Hollywood

    Before she met Keanu, Jennifer Syme was already rubbing elbows with plenty of Hollywood greats. In fact, one of her first gigs was working for director David Lynch.

    After that, Jennifer worked as a personal assistant to a handful of famous folks, including rocker Dave Navarro. Working behind the scenes wasn't her only goal, though; Syme also appeared on screen.

    Syme played a "junkie girl" in a 1997 film, also acting as a production assistant on the film. She would later receive a production assistant credit for one episode of a TV show, then was posthumously credited in a 2005 film in which she played "casting chick."

    Keanu Reeves' Girlfriend Knew Marilyn Manson

    Jennifer was, apparently, well-connected enough to make friends with various celebrities, including Marilyn Manson. In fact, the night she passed away, she had just gotten home from (and was apparently returning to) Marilyn's house, where a party was happening.

    RELATED: One Fan Who Dated Him Says This Is What Keanu Reeves Is Really Like In Person

    Syme's mother later filed charges against Manson, alleging that it was his fault Jennifer drove while under the influence. In fact, Syme's mother accused Manson of encouraging her daughter to get in her car and drive that night.

    As many already know, Syme passed away after crashing her Jeep into various parked vehicles.

    Reports later confirmed that she was under the influence during the accident, and was also not wearing a seatbelt. However, Manson denied any wrongdoing or involvement at all in Jennifer's passing.

    Jennifer, who a friend of Keanu's called "sweet," was thrown from the vehicle and apparently died on impact.

    Keanu Learned Jennifer Had Passed Away By Calling The Coroner

    At least one source reported that Keanu found out that his ex-girlfriend had passed away by calling the local coroner's office the morning after her accident.

    Many fans found it interesting that Keanu would be checking up on his ex, but as it turns out, the two had only seen one another the day prior. The two had brunch together the day before Keanu would call the coroner looking for the mother of his child.

    Despite their breakup, Jennifer and Keanu were apparently still close, though grief over the loss of their stillborn baby allegedly caused their split.

    After Syme's passing, a friend told journalists that Keanu was "finding it very, very difficult to cope," which later led to his reputation of having a traumatic past.

    Jennifer Syme Was Dealing With Personal Struggles

    News sources confirmed that Syme had been treated for depression shortly before her passing, but also that she had two prescriptions in the vehicle, as well as a white powdery substance, upon her death.

    Fans may recall that late 1999 was when Syme gave birth to her daughter with Keanu, and the pair split a few weeks later. However, the accident occurred in 2001, with Syme passing away in April.

    Jennifer was buried next to her daughter, sources confirm, leaving Keanu "heartbroken" and "in pain." And for a long time, no one really knew whether Keanu was dating anyone.

    Plus, over the years, "sad Keanu" memes have surfaced, perpetuating the idea that Keanu has been sad and alone ever since his ex-girlfriend passed away. While he clearly mourned her and the two were close, Keanu seems to be doing well enough in life these days.

    RELATED: Here’s How Keanu Reeves Spends His $360 Million Net Worth

    He maintains a positive outlook on life and fame and often donates time and money to worthwhile causes. Not to mention, Keanu seems to have found love with Alexandra Grant, who helped him create two books featuring her photographs and his poetry.

    Despite the tragedies in his earlier life, Keanu is still an icon to fans, both for his acting repertoire and how wholesome he seems as a human in general. From his kind attitude toward fans to his willingness to live like an average person rather than a millionaire super-celeb, there are plenty of reasons why fans love Keanu.

    And it's nice to see him happy with someone, especially because not many of his relationships have been publicized. Yes, his relationships are his business -- but fans are glad he finally found some happiness after losing someone he loved.

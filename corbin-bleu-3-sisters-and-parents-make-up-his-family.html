    Corbin Bleu sisters are Phoenix Reivers, Hunter Reivers and Jag Reivers. The Bleu siblings were born and raised in New York City.

    The American actor and singer Bleu is best known for his lead role as Chad Danforth in the High School Musical trilogy (2006-2008).

    He debuted in the adventure comedy Catch That Kid (2004), and began acting professionally in the early 2000s. Corbin appeared in the Discovery Kids drama series Fight 29 Down (2005-2007).

    Bleu also starred in the popular Disney Channel Original Movie Jump In! in 2007 and the film To Write Love on Her Arms in 2015.

    The actor has also pursued a music career and released his debut album, Another Side, in 2007, which included the hit pop/R&B song Push It to the Limit.

    His album peaked at No. 36 on the U.S. Billboard 200 album chart and sold 18,000 copies in its first week. He released his second and final studio album, Speed of Light, in 2009.

    Blew then returned to television, landing starring roles in the short-lived CW series The Beautiful Life: TBL (2009) and the coming-of-age film Free Style (2009).

    Corbin Grew Up With Strong Female Influences

    Corbin Bleu sisters Phoenix, Jag, and Hunter Reivers are younger than him. Their great-uncle is actor Joseph Callari from Modern Family.

    Born to David Reivers and Martha Callari, the Bleu siblings were born and raised in New York City, in the borough of Brooklyn.

    The actor is extremely close to his family. He even dedicated his performance in Dancing with the Stars Season 17 (2013) to his siblings.

    “I have a very close, tight-knit family,” Corbin Bleu said.

    Phoenix Reivers

    Phoenix is currently pursuing her Bachelor’s degree in Aerospace, Aeronautical, and Astronautical Engineering at Stanford University.

    A proud brother, Bleu took to his Instagram on May 1, 2019, to share he is incredibly proud of his brilliant sister. “She will officially be studying aerospace engineering at Stanford University!!!” he added.

    From January 2020 to July 2020, Phoenix co-leaded the Project Phoenix Structures at Stanford Student Space Initiative.

    She has also served as Project Nebula co-lead and Rockets Team co-lead. In January 2022, she joined the Reconfigurable and Active Structures Lab as an undergraduate research assistant.

    In August 2022, she again joined Stanford Student Space Initiative as Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion co-lead. She will graduate from the university in 2023.

    Jag Reivers

    Jag is studying nursing at Azusa Pacific University (APU), a private university in Azusa, California, United States.

    She was born in August 2003 and is 19 years old. On August 18, 2013, her brother marked her 10th birthday by sharing a loving picture of all the siblings on Twitter.

    “Celebrating my sis Jag’s 10th Birthday with my other sister’s Phoenix & @LoveHunter33 @ Pirate Show in Buena Park CA,” he tweeted.

    Reivers is active on Instagram under the username @jagyoureit, with ten posts and 1.1k followers. Her IG account is private, but she can also be found on TikTok by jag_reivers, with over 1.4k likes.

    She usually shares body positivity posts on her account. She also uses the platform to share her life as a nursing student with her patient.

    Hunter Reivers

    Hunter graduated from John Burroughs High School, Burbank, California, in May 2011. She then joined Baylor University that fall and graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Biology in May 2015.

    Hunter is available on Instagram under the username @thehuntergrey, with 87 posts and 818 followers.

    Meet Corbin Bleu Parents

    Bleu is the son of Martha Callari and David Reivers. His dad David is an actor and greatly influences Corbin’s life.

    As a child, he studied dance for several years, especially Ballet and Jazz. At age two, he appeared in TV commercials for products such as Bounty, Nabisco, Life cereal, and Hasbro.

    David Reivers

    Corbin Bleu father David is a Jamaican-American actor. He is best known for co-starring as his real-life son’s father in Jump In! and High School Musical 3: Senior Year.

    He also had a recurring role as Bob Cowan in the American fantasy drama series Charmed (2001-2002). The 64-year-old has been in the entertainment industry since 1989.

    According to his IMDb bio, he made his acting debut appearing as Steven in Forbidden Fruit and Dan in the TV series As the World Turns in 1989.

    In 1990, he landed the role of Airline Representative in Agnes Nixon’s All My Children. The same year, he appeared as a father in Say No to Strangers and Young Gabriel Bird in Gabriel’s Fire.

    Other notable credits include Reading Rainbow (1996), Home Improvement (1999), City of Angels (2000), The Guardian (2002), Gilmore Girls (2005), Poseidon (2006), Family Tools (2013), and The Rookie (2021).

    Mother Martha Callari Reivers

    Corbin Bleu mother Martha is Italian American. She reportedly worked as a Special Education Instructor for Adults at Parks and Rec Department in the Greater New York City Area.

    In November 2013, she came to Dancing with the Stars to cheer her son. The actor also shared a beautiful picture of himself with his loving mother on Facebook and Instagram.

    “With my beautiful Mom, Martha Reivers, in the #DWTS Ballroom! #Finale Thanks to all the fans who voted,” he wrote in the caption.

    The Actor Appeared in Say Yes To The Dress

    Bleu, alongside his fiance Sasha Clements, appeared in Say Yes to the Dress Season 14 Episode 3 on March 10, 2016.

    Clements, a Canadian actress known for playing Kiki Kincaid on Majority Rules!, arrived at Kleinfeld Bridal with her mom, aunt, best friend, little brother, and Bleu to find her dream dress.

    And while she searched for a classic ball gown, the actor wanted something more daring.

    The pair married on July 23, 2016, at Hummingbird Best in Santa Susana. The bride wore a custom Pnina Tornai gown, while the groom and his groomsmen wore gray Portofino tuxedos.

    Bleu met his life partner, Clements for the first time in a grocery aisle of a supermarket where the actress used to work in 2011.

    The actor planned a Disney proposal to take their relationship further as her girl was an avid Harry Potter and Disney Lover.

    He took her to Disney World on the pretext that they had to do a photo shoot and interview in front of Cinderella Castle. But later, he sat on one knee, holding an engagement ring and popping the question.

    The overwhelmed Sasha said yes. And right after he asked her and she said yes, fireworks went on, people were cheering, and they stayed in the castle to mark the memorable day.

    Born in Toronto, Ontario, Corbin Bleu wife Sasha Clements is the daughter of Anna Clements and Christopher Lee Clements, an actor in the Canadian musical drama series Catwalk.

    She landed her first starring role in the Canadian TV series Majority Rules! in 2009, portraying 15-year-old Kiki. She then appeared in What’s Up Warthogs! in 2010 and Lost Girl in 2012.

    Other acting credits include How to Build a Better Boy (2014), Open Heart (2015), Degrassi: The Next Generation (2015), Love, for Real (2021), A Christmas Dance Reunion (2021), and Minx (2022).

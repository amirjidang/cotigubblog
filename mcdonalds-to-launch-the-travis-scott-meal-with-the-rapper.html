    McDonald's to Launch the 'Travis Scott Meal' With the Rapper

    Step aside, Happy Meal, theres a new spread in town! McDonalds has joined forces with Travis Scott (whose nickname is Cactus Jack) and plans to launch a new menu item with the rapper for a limited time.

    31 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Step aside, Happy Meal, there’s a new spread in town! McDonald’s has joined forces with Travis Scott (whose nickname is Cactus Jack) and plans to launch a new menu item with the rapper for a limited time.

    “Now two icons are coming together, as The Golden Arches meet Cactus Jack for a multi-level innovative partnership,” McDonald’s said in a press release shared on Thursday, September 3. “From September 8th through October 4th, McDonald’s is taking it up to another level, as cultural sensation Travis Scott’s favorite meal is offered at restaurants all across the U.S.”

    According to the release, the “Travis Scott Meal,” as it has been dubbed, will include a beef Quarter Pounder with cheese, bacon and lettuce, a medium French fries with BBQ sauce to dip, as well as a Sprite. It will cost approximately $6.

    “I couldn’t be more excited to bring the Cactus Jack x McDonald’s collaboration to life,” Scott, 28, said in a statement. “We are bringing together two iconic worlds. Including a charitable component was key for me, and I can’t wait for people to see what we have in store.”

    As the “Butterfly Effect” rapper implied, his limited-edition meal will also give back to others by “exploring opportunities to support charitable organizations during the month-long program.”

    Additionally, Cactus Jack has designed custom apparel for McDonald’s crew.

    The Texas native has been teasing this collaboration for weeks and confirmed the news on Thursday when he tweeted a photo of McDonald’s French fries. “CACTUS JACK FOR MCDONALD’S,” he wrote along with the snapshot.

    Scott is only the second celebrity to ever have his name featured on the McDonald’s menu. The first was basketball legend Michael Jordan, who joined forces with the fast-food chain in 1992 to release the McJordan Special value meal.

    “Travis is a true McDonald’s fan, having grown up visiting our restaurants in Houston, not to mention one of the biggest musical acts and cultural icons in the world,” Morgan Flatley, McDonald’s U.S. chief marketing officer, explained. “This is the first time in nearly 30 years we’ve introduced a superstar’s name to our menu – we’re excited to bring the ‘Travis Scott Meal’ to a McDonald’s near you starting next week.”

    To snag the “Travis Scott Meal,” fans can order it in-restaurant, for carry-out, at the drive thru or via the McDonald’s app starting on Tuesday, September 8.

    This isn’t the first food collaboration Scott has participated in. In June 2019, he teamed up with Reese’s Puffs cereal to release a limited-edition box as well as some themed apparel for the brand. That venture proved to be a smashing success, as the cereal boxes sold out less than 30 seconds after they went on sale.

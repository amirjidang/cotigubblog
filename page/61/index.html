Wolfgang Schuble Obituary

27 Aug 2024, 00:00

Wolfgang Schäuble, a stalwart German politician, minister, and statesman, whose remarkable political journey spanned over five decades, bid farewell on December 26, 2023, succumbing to cancer at the age of 81, surrounded by his family. Born in Freiburg in 1942, Schäuble emerged as a leading figure within the conservative Christian Democrats (CDU). His political odyssey began in 1972 when he entered parliament, and he went on to secure victory in his Offenburg constituency in every subsequent election, earning the distinction of being Germany’s longest-serving postwar parliamentarian.

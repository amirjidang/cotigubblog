    Career

    Teutul is an American television personality, best known for his appearances on the Discovery Channel reality show Orange County Choppers, from 2002 to 2009. He is the son of Paul Teutul Sr., the founder of Orange County Choppers.

    Teutul started working at Orange County Choppers when he was 14 years old, beginning out with menial tasks like answering phones and taking out the trash. He finally worked his way up to become an assistant general manager and started building motorcycles himself.

    Orange County Choppers became the priority of the reality show American Chopper, which premiered on the Discovery Channel in 2002. The show followed the day-to-day operations of the shop, and the often-strained relationship between Paul Sr. and Paul Jr. Teutul was featured prominently on the show, and his laid-back personality and comedic jokes made him a fan dearest.

    Teutul quit Orange County Choppers in 2009, following a falling out with his father. He shortly appeared on the spin-off show American Chopper: Senior vs. Junior, which pitted Paul Sr. and Paul Jr. against each other in a motorcycle-building contest.

    In recent years, Teutul has pursued a variety of experiences, such as forming his motorcycle shop, establishing a line of gourmet pasta sauces, and appearing in reality shows like Celebrity Ghost Stories and Celebrity Wife Swap.

    Megan Fox rose to fame in the early 2000s for her roles in blockbuster films like Transformers and Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles. With her striking looks and undeniable on-screen charisma, Fox quickly became one of Hollywood's most sought-after talents. Her net worth is estimated to be around $8 million, allowing her to indulge in her passion for luxury automobiles.

    BMW X5 - $78,990

    One of the jewels in Megan Fox's car collection is the BMW X5. With its sleek design and powerful performance, this SUV perfectly complements her glamorous persona. Priced at $78,990, the BMW X5 boasts a robust 3.0-liter six-cylinder engine, generating an impressive 335 horsepower. Equipped with advanced technology and luxurious features, such as a panoramic sunroof and leather upholstery, the BMW X5 offers both comfort and style for Megan Fox's daily travels.

    Mercedes-Benz CLS 55 AMG - $86,600

    Megan Fox added a stunning black Mercedes-Benz CLS 55 AMG to her car collection. With a price tag of $86,600, this sedan exudes sophistication and power. The CLS 55 AMG is equipped with a 4.7-liter Twin Turbo V8 engine, producing an exhilarating 402 horsepower, according to Car HP. Its sleek and aerodynamic design also boasts an interior featuring premium leather seats and a state-of-the-art infotainment system, making it a perfect choice for Megan Fox's refined taste.

    Range Rover Sport - $110,900

    The Range Rover Sport adds rugged capability to Fox's garage. As an SUV built for adventure, this Land Rover model easily handles off-road terrain while cosseting passengers in a richly appointed cabin. The Sport's sophisticated air suspension delivers a smooth ride on- or off-road. Fox can enjoy massaging seats, tri-zone climate control, a rear seat entertainment system, and a 1700-watt Meridian sound system inside. With abundant power from its supercharged V8 engine, the Range Rover Sport is ideal for conquering challenging trails or cruising streets in refined comfort.

    Range Rover Sport SVR - $125,000

    Taking the Range Rover Sport to new heights, Megan Fox also possesses a metallic gray Range Rover Sport SVR, priced at $125,000. This high-performance SUV pushes the boundaries of speed. Powered by a supercharged 5.0-liter V8 engine, the Range Rover Sport SVR unleashes a remarkable 518 horsepower, propelling it from 0 to 60 mph in just 5.1 seconds. Its aggressive exterior styling, enhanced aerodynamics, and sport-tuned suspension make it a thrilling ride.

    Tesla Model X - $135,890

    Embracing the future of automotive technology, Megan Fox added a stunning white Tesla Model X to her collection. Offering room for seven, this fully-electric SUV can silently whisk Megan and her family around in an eco-friendly style. Its dual motor AWD system provides instant torque for a thrilling 3.8-second 0-60 MPH time. It offers up to 289 miles of range on a single charge, as per TMC. The Model X's huge panoramic windshield and falcon wing doors make for a distinctive look. As an added bonus, Tesla's Autopilot system enables semi-autonomous driving capabilities.

    Mercedes-Benz G550 AMG - $145,900

    With its boxy silhouette and three locking differentials, the Mercedes-Benz G-Class is built for adventure. Underneath its rugged exterior, the G550 AMG packs a hand-built biturbo 4.0-liter V8 churning out 416 horsepower as per 21 Motoring. This old-school off-roader retains an iconic design, offering modern amenities like heated/cooled seats, ambient lighting, and COMAND infotainment. With the G550 AMG, Fox's powerful SUV is ready to tackle dirt roads in comfort and style.

    Mercedes-Benz G63 AMG - $156,450

    The Mercedes-Benz G63 AMG flaunts a hand-crafted biturbo 5.5-liter V8 producing 577 horsepower. With sport-tuned suspension and AMG enhancements, this boxy SUV delivers astonishing performance. The G63 AMG rockets from 0-60 mph in just 4.4 seconds. Beyond its potent engine, the G63 pampers occupants with Designo Nappa leather seats, carbon fiber trim, and the latest Mercedes infotainment.

    Aston Martin DB11 - $210,900

    Priced at $210,900, this grand tourer features a hand-crafted 5.2-liter twin-turbo V12 engine that unleashes 600 horsepower through an 8-speed automatic gearbox capable of taking the DB11 to a top speed of 208 MPH, as per Car and Driver. The interior is a masterpiece of craftsmanship, with hand-stitched leather seats, exquisite wood trim, and cutting-edge technology, providing a great driving experience.

    Range Rover SV Autobiography HSE - $220,000

    Completing Megan Fox's spectacular car collection is the Range Rover SV Autobiography HSE, priced at $220,000. This flagship SUV offers uncompromising performance and off-road capability. The Autobiography is powered by a supercharged 5.0-liter V8 engine, producing an impressive 557 horsepower. Its elegant and timeless design, an opulent interior featuring premium leather upholstery, an advanced infotainment system, and refined comfort features add to its value.

    With her mix of sporty coupes, SUVs, and exotic supercars, Megan Fox's car collection provides a glimpse into her lavish lifestyle. Her penchant for luxury vehicles is clearly stated through her extensive garage.

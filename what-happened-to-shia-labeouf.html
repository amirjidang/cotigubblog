    Shia LaBeouf Was Confirmed Into The Catholic Church

    Shia LaBeouf considers taking up a Deacon position in the Catholic Church

    If fans were wondering what happened to Shia LaBeouf, it seems he is considering taking up a post in the Catholic Church. It was revealed that Shia LaBeouf has been confirmed in the Catholic Church.

    "We are thrilled to share that our dear friend Shia LaBeouf has fully entered the Church this past weekend through the sacrament of confirmation," The statement released by the Capuchin Franciscans-Western American Province church said.

    "Shia LaBeouf, known for his incredible talent and passion in the entertainment industry, has embarked on a profound spiritual journey that has led him to embrace the teachings of the Catholic Church... His decision to fully enter the Church is a testament to his sincere desire to grow in his relationship with God and live out the Gospel values."

    According to Capuchin friar Brother Alexander Rodriguez, who served as LaBeouf's confirmation sponsor, LaBeouf is also thinking of becoming a deacon someday.

    This is a milestone for the Fury actor, but the move is not surprising. Shia LaBeouf has shown interest in Christianity and growing in faith.

    After starring in the biographical film Padre Pio in 2022, about the titular priest who received the stigmata after his contribution to the Catholic faith. Working on the Saint changed LaBeouf's worldview, which was what led him down this new path.

    As he spent time learning about Pio, he found new meaning and inspiration from the life of the Italian Saint.

    "If you like immersive experiences, and you get tasked with playing Pio, your life is going to change," LaBeouf said.

    His newfound commitment to Christianity and Catholicism began to shape up, and now, Shia LaBeouf is continuing on his new path.

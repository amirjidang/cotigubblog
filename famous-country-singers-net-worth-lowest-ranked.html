    These Famous Country Singers Don't Have Nearly As Much Money As You Think

    Country singers are some of the wealthiest musicians in the world. Icons like Dolly Parton and Shania Twain have net worths over $300 million - but it takes years upon years of success to acquire that type of money, not just millions of Instagram followers.

    RELATED: 10 Country Singers Who Are Sweethearts (And 10 Who Are Secretly Jerks)

    Most of the country musicians who are popular today, while of course, they're successful, they may not have as much money as you think they do. Let's take a look at some famous country singers whose bank accounts are slightly smaller than imagined.

    8 What Is Sam Hunt's Net Worth?

    Sam Hunt has been an active member of the country music scene for years. After releasing his debut album Montevallo, the singer skyrocketed to fame, but he'd been involved behind the scenes for years. Sam first started gaining recognition within the country music world as a songwriter. He's written singles for Reba McEntire, Kenny Chesney, and Keith Urban. His solo career, songwriting, and production have helped him accumulate his $3 million net worth.

    7 How Much Is Jimmie Allen's Net Worth?

    Country music star Jimmie Allen's net worth is around $2.5 million. The singer's career has not been easy, experiencing poverty and frequently living out of his car his first few years living in Nashville. But while on American Idol, Jimmie befriended Scotty McCreery. Scotty would go on to win the show and invite Jimmie to tour with him afterward.

    Jimmie released his debut album before the tour. In 2021, Jimmie won the Country Music Association Award for New Artist of the Year. He's the second black man to do so, with Darius Rucker being the first.

    6 What Is Gabby Barrett Foehner's Net Worth?

    Gabby Barrett Foehner's net worth is around $1 million. The country singer has an annual income of roughly $250,000. She first made headlines after appearing on the 16th season of American Idol. She released her debut album in 2020, with Country Now calling her the next big country superstar. Her debut single, I Hope, was the first single by an unaccompanied woman to top the Top 10 Hot Country Songs list since 2017.

    Gabby also fell in love with her fellow American Idol competitor, Cade Foehner. The two are happily married and have two children together. While Gabby is focused on being the best mother, she's been writing, singing, and touring simultaneously.

    5 Colter Wall Has A Net Worth of $5 Million

    Colter Wall has accumulated a net worth of $4 million since the release of his self-titled debut album in 2017. The Canadian crooner has had his music featured in numerous films and television shows, such as the Oscar-winning film Three Billboards Outside Ebbing Missouri. He's still working on new projects and has a few shows scheduled through the United States in 2022 and early 2023.

    4 What Is Carley Pearce's Net Worth?

    Carley has been singing since she was a teenager, but when she moved to Nashville at 19, she started to get noticed. After years of networking in the country scene, she secured a feature on Wasn't That Drunk, a track by the Josh Abbott Band. Her voice could be heard on nearly every top country station, and she was even invited to perform with the group on Jimmy Kimmel Live.

    RELATED: These Country Music Stars Live Off The Map

    In 2021, Carley released her third studio album, 29: Written In Stone, helping to solidify her win at the 2022 ACM awards for Female Artist of the Year. She signed with Big Machine Records that year and has been on a trajectory toward the moon since. As of today, her net worth sits at around $3 Million.

    3 Cody Jinks Has A New Worth Of $2.5 Million

    Cody Jinks has been a staple within the country world since his 2016 breakout album I'm Not The Devil. His last two albums were released within one week of each other, with both albums reaching the Number 1 and 2 spot on the Top Albums charts. He's continued to produce and release his albums independently. His net worth as of 2022 is around $2.5 million.

    2 How Much Is Walker Hayes Net Worth?

    Walker Hayes found success years after moving to Nashville to pursue his passion for country music. He almost quit country music because of how rocky the road was. Many fans know him for his hit song "Feel Like," which topped the Billboard Hot Country Chart in 2021 and 2022. Walker, however, has been working on his craft since 2006.

    RELATED: How Toby Keith Became One Of Country's Biggest Stars

    Through years of songwriting, producing, and bouncing between different labels, Walker's first taste of billboard success was in 2016 with his single You Broke Up With Me. His previous work has yet to take off, like Fancy Like, becoming a viral TikTok song that is still featured prominently on the app today. His net worth as of 2022 is around $4.5 Million.

    1 What Is Lauren Alaina's Net Worth?

    Lauren Alaina has been hitting the road hard since coming in second place during the 10th season of American Idol. The singer and songwriter's latest album, Sitting Pretty On Top Of The World, was released in September 2021. Before that, she had released numerous singles and two studio albums.

    In December 2021, Lauren was invited by singer Trisha Yearwood to join the Grand Ole Opry. As of 2022, her net worth is estimated between $800,000 to $1 Million.

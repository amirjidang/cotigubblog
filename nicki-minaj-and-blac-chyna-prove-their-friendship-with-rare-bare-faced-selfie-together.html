    Kardashians who? While their rivals scramble over divorce rumors and parenting drama, Nicki Minaj and Blac Chyna just showed the world that they are doing just fine.

    Across their socials this morning, Nicki, Chyna, and the Barbz were sharing a shot of the pair spending time together in Beverly Hills looking completely unbothered. Usually these women are all about the glam, but this time, they're going natural.

    'The Barbie and the Chyna Doll'

    Nicki was the first to share this pic, followed by Blac Chyna a few hours later on her IG. It shows them both glowing with smooth, natural faces and either very minimal makeup (lipgloss on Nicki?) or no makeup at all.

    Fans admired how fresh-faced and young they looked, with IG influencer @arii commenting "that natural beauty" and actress Skai Jackson writing "Wow you look like a teenager, so pretty."

    "You guys always stun together. Both of you are glowing," agreed one fan, with another commenting "you literally never age."

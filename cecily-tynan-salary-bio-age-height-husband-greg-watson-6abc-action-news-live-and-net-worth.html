    Cecily Tynan Biography

    Cecily Tynan is an American Television Meteorologist, Broadcast Television Anchor, and Journalist who was born and brought-up in Newtown, Connecticut. Currently, she works at WPVI-TV 6abc Action News in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania as the Chief Meteorologist and the 5, 6, and 11 pm Weathercaster. She joined the 6abc weather team, working as the Weekend Weather Anchor and a General Assignment Reporter.

    Previously, Cecily hosted ‘Primetime Weekend’, the Saturday evening public affairs program. Prior to joining the weather team in October 1995, she worked KTNV in Las Vegas, Nevada as a weather and news anchor for Good Morning Las Vegas. She worked at WDBJ in Roanoke, Virginia as the weekend weather anchor and a general assignment reporter. She has received multiple awards and honors which include “Best On-Air Talent,” Las Vegas Electronic Media Awards; and “Best Weathercaster,” Las Vegas Review Journal.

    Cecily Tynan Career

    Cecily Tynan ABC News- WPVI-TV

    Tynan works for WPVI-TV since joining the station in 1995. She is currently a weathercaster for 5, 6, and 11 pm and also the WPVI chief meteorologist. Tynan previously hosted the Saturday evening public affairs program Primetime Weekend show which was hosted by Gary Papa until his death on June 19, 2009.

    After graduation from Washington and Lee University, the American Meteorological society employed her as a weather forecaster. After a while working there, she moved to Las Vegas where she worked as a weather and a news anchor for Good Morning Las Vegas station.

    WDBJ tv station then hired her as a morning news anchor and an assignment reporter. Cecily was later on shifted to be a morning weather reporter. During her tenure at WDBJ, she co-hosted several shows such as Philly on the Wheels and The Flower Show. She was later promoted for her good work to be the chief meteorologist for WBDJ Tv.

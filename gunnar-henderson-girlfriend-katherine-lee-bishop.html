    Gunnar Henderson And Katherine Relationship Timeline

    Gunnar has been dating Kate since 2019. The dazzling lovers recently celebrated third anniversary of their blooming relationship.

    Both of them hail from the same state of Alabama and also attended the same Auburn University. Henderson knows Katherine from her high school days. 

    They fell in love as they learned more about each other and spent quality time together. It didn’t take long for the Barioles infielder to know she was the one for him.

    Henderson introduced his partner in social media for the first time on November 2, 2019, with an Instagram post. 

    He shared a photo of him alongside Kat with the caption, “Well we didn’t go boggin’ on the plains, but a football game will do!” They opted to watch a football match instead of going to the recreation center in Alabama. 

    Fourteen days later, G posted some cute photos of them together. He relished a light moment with a photo from a party stating his reaction when Kat thinks her dance moves are better than his.

    Bishop also posted a cute photo of them smiling to the fullest and savoring the moment on November 23, 2019 on her Facebook account.

    The rising baseball star wished a happy birthday to his favorite girl on December 29, 2019, with the “love you” caption.

    Celebration of Second Anniversary

    Katherine Bishop Studies Pharmacy

    Katherine was born on December 20, 1999 in Thomasville, Alabama. She grew up in her hometown alongside her brother Logan, father Todd, and mother Lee Bishop.

    Lee went to Thomasville Middle School, where she emerged as an intelligent student after winning the elementary school award. She also competed in the 2014 Hope Pageant and became the runner-up. 

    Gunnar’s partner excelled in multiple facets and arose as a comprehensive package from her childhood. She also played volleyball and softball and succeeded in track during high school.

    Katherine is currently studying at the Auburn University Harrison College of Pharmacy. She is in her third year and aims to earn a doctorate in pharmacy by 2025. She has begun brightly, with a perfect 4.0 GPA in the first year.

    Although she is active on Instagram @katherinelee_bishop, she has kept it private and has fewer than 5000 followers. She has shared many photos on her Facebook account, including those from her high school athletic days.

    Gunnar Henderson Family Life

    Gunnar Henderson was born to parents AllenHenderson and Kerry Henderson  on June 29, 2001. He grew up in Selma, Alabama.

    He attended John T. Morgan Academy during his childhood. He was raised in a household with a high passion for baseball, that they built a baseball field in the backyard.

    He is a passionate Auburn Tigers fan, along with his entire family. Unsurprisingly, he committed to playing collegiate baseball at Auburn University. Henderson instantly made his mark in his senior year as the Alabama Player of the Year 2019.

    The multi-talented athlete grew up playing various sports apart from baseball, like football, basketball, and soccer. He was equally capable in basketball as well.

    Gunnar was named the Alabama Independent School Association Basketball Player of the Year for his stellar record of 17 points and 11 rebounds per game.

    Henderson Parents are Baseball Fanatics

    Love and enthusiasm for the game run deep in the veins of Henderson’s old folks. The Baltimore Orioles star was inspired to play baseball by his father since childhood.

    Gunnar’s dad Allen Henderson is a massive fan of the Auburn Tigers. His mom Kerry initially supported the Alabama Crimson Tide baseball team.

    In an interview with The Athletic, Gunnar revealed an interesting story about how that changed later. Kerry started supporting the Auburn Tigers after her marriage with Allen.

    Both Allen and Kerry are very supportive of their son and often present in the stadium to cheer for him during the match days. 

    Gunnar’s Brothers Also Play Baseball

    Gunnar Henderson has two brothers, Jackson Henderson, and Cade Henderson. Jackson is the elder one, whereas Cade is the youngest. 

    The three siblings are very close to each other as they were raised together and all share the same interest in sports.

    Jackson was also interested in baseball from a very early age and was the first to be trained by their father. He played college baseball at Lurleen B. Wallace Community College in Alabama. He also made it to the Auburn University baseball team later.

    Like his older brothers, Cade plays high school baseball at Morgan Academy. He is a massive fan of the Baltimore Orioles. The young lad even wanted to wear his Orioles pajamas to Gunnar’s draft signing, which their mom declined.

    Gunnar firmly believes Cade also has the potential to succeed, and there’s every possibility that the two brothers might be playing MLB together five years down the lane.

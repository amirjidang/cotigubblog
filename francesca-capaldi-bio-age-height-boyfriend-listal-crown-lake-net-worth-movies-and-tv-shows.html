    Francesca Capaldi Career

    Speaking of her career, Capaldi’s passion for the screenplays began when she was a child when she was first given small roles in the Disney Channel Original Series A.N.T. Farm and in the CBS comedy How I Met Your Mother, as a 7-year-old Lily, who is played as an adult by Alyson Hannigan.

    In 2019, she began starring in the Brat series Crown Lake. Capaldi has also starred in Corbin Bernsen’s independent film 3 Day Test and Dog with a Blog on Disney Channel, her series-regular debut. Before staring in films, she had a life long best friend Jada Del Rio.

    Although Capaldi states they try to keep in touch as much as possible it still gets difficult at times seeing Jada’s moved to New York and Capaldi stays in San Diego California. In the 2015 film The Peanuts Movie. Francesca voices the Little Red-Haired Girl—that was her first voicing career.

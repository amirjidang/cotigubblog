And as it turns out, the billionaire now has now fathered 10 children (which is a little more than Nick Cannon’s own brood). Naturally, this only stirred up more questions, with many wondering why Zilis never gave her children Musk’s last name upon their birth in the first place.

    How Did Shivon Zilis End Up Having Elon's Babies?

    In the machine intelligence landscape, Zilis is, arguably, one of the best and brightest. In 2017, Zilis joined Tesla as a project director. According to her LinkedIn page, she worked on AI projects, mainly working with the company’s autopilot and chip design team. She has also become an advisor to OpenAI, a nonprofit research laboratory for artificial intelligence that Musk co-founded.

    Around the same time she joined Tesla, Zilis was also named the director of operations and special projects at Musk’s other company, Neuralink. In recent months, Neuralink has come under fire after it acknowledged that monkeys had died during its testing procedure, which includes inserting implantable chips into the animals’ brains.

    “It is important to note that these accusations come from people who oppose any use of animals in research,” the company wrote on its website. “Currently, all novel medical devices and treatments must be tested in animals before they can be ethically trialed in humans. Neuralink is not unique in this regard.”

    Beyond work, however, it didn’t appear that Musk and Zilis were ever romantically involved as many were only aware of the billionaire’s relationships with Grimes and actress Amber Heard in recent years. That said, Zilis has always been supportive of Musk and his various business ventures on social media.

    And even after news broke regarding their twins, Zilis has never spoken out or posted anything on social media about having children with Musk (although her Twitter feed clearly shows that she loves children).

    That said, the billionaire did acknowledge on Twitter that his brood just gotten larger, explaining, “I'm trying to set a good example! Population collapse is a much bigger problem than people realize and that's just for Earth. Mars has a great need for people, seeing as population is currently zero.”

    Why Didn’t Shivon Zilis Give Her Twins Elon Musk’s Name At Birth?

    The truth is no one would ever really know unless Zilis herself speaks up. However, that will likely not happen as she doesn’t ever discuss her private life. At the same time, Zilis may have also wanted to keep things under the radar as she raised her newborn babies, cleverly avoiding the media circus that ensued when Musk’s first child with Grimes, X, was born. “We move and move and move because people keep finding where we live,” Grimes once said.

    Do Elon Musk And Shivon Zilis Live Together?

    The now-public filing may also indicate that Zilis and Musk have plans to raise their young family together with the papers revealing that both stated the same address in Austin as their primary residence. Zilis was previously living in San Francisco but moved to Austin three months before giving birth.

    Meanwhile, Musk was supposedly residing in a modular home in Boca Chica. At the same time, while he was still with Grimes, Musk also said that he prefers living separately, elaborating, “I just don’t like things to be messy and anime.”

    As for Musk and Zilis, no one really knows where their relationship stands today with neither of them ever acknowledging romantically in public. Interestingly, however, Zilis once retweeted a video of Musk’s Starship with the caption, “Even Starships need a hug sometimes.”

    When it comes to Elon and Grimes, the last comment the latter gave was, “There’s no real word for it. I would probably refer to him as my boyfriend, but we’re very fluid.”

    She elaborated, “We live in separate houses. We’re best friends. We see each other all the time…. We just have our own thing going on, and I don’t expect other people to understand it.”

    It is also unclear if Grimes and Musk plan to have more children together in the future, though they may still be on good terms despite Musk having fathered more children with someone else.

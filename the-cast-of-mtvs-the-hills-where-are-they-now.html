    20 Aug 2024, 00:00

    The Hills is easily one of the most successful reality television series to ever grace our screens! The show first began back in 2006 with Lauren Conrad, Whitney Port, Heidi Montag, and Audrina Patridge as the cast's main character. The show went on to run for over 6 seasons and paved the way for countless other shows to follow suit, including Whitney Port's MTV spin-off, The City. Heidi and Lauren's mega feud was one of the biggest storylines to come out of 'The Hills', making it one of the top contenders at the time.

    The show has since come to an end, however, MTV announced the official reboot, The Hills: New Beginnings, which has led many fans to question whether the show is real or fake. Despite its legitimacy, it has been a great way to see what some of our favorites have been up to. Lauren, Lo, Kristin, and Kelly Cutrone did not appear in the revival, leaving many to wonder where they are now.

    10 Lauren Conrad

    Lauren Conrad is easily the breakout star from MTV's The Hills. The TV personality turn fashion designer began her career out on the hit show, Laguna Beach that is until she got her own spin-off in 2006. While Lauren may have left the show halfway through the fifth season, it wouldn't be the last we saw of Conrad.

    Related: The Hills Cast Ranked By Net Worth

    The star went on to become a New York Times best-selling author, a fashion designer, and more recently a beauty company CEO! Lauren launched her brand new company, Lauren Conrad Beauty in 2020, and continues to take over the world!

    9 Whitney Port

    Whitney Port worked alongside Lauren Conrad in the iconic Teen Vogue closet. The Hills star was known to be the down to Earth sweet girl, a reputation she has since kept up with ever since her debut 15 years ago. Whitney went on to get her own MTV spin-off, The City, where she began her own fashion label, Whitney Eve.

    Since then, Port got married to MTV producer, Tim Rosenmann, with whom she shares their 3-year-old son, Sonny Rosenmann. Whitney and Tim now have a super-popular YouTube channel where they have been re-watching and reacting to both The City, and The Hills all while giving some major inside scoop!

    8 Heidi & Spencer

    Heidi Montag and Spencer Pratt may have become the villains of the show, the duo has since redeemed themselves on the most recent reboot of the hit reality series, The Hills: New Beginnings. The couple, who are still very much into crystals and everything luxurious, have since become parents! Heidi gave birth to her and Spencer's son, Gunner Pratt, back on October 1, 2017.

    Heidi has spoken out regarding her issues surrounding motherhood and how difficult it was to separate herself from her baby boy. Despite their mean streak on the original series, Speidi appears to be far more mature than when we were first introduced to them.

    7 Audrina Patridge

    Audrina Patridge struck gold when Adam DiVello came across her at The Villas pool, the residences in which Lauren and Heidi lived for the first two seasons of the show. Patridge became a leading lady for all 6 seasons and has since reprised her role on the reboot. In 2016, Audrina married her boyfriend, Corey Bohan, who appeared on a number of episodes of The Hills. Although they made for a picture-perfect couple, the duo split in 2018! Audrina now focuses mainly on the former couple's daughter, Kirra Bohan, all while readying the filming of season two of The Hills: New Beginnings.

    6 Lo Bosworth

    Lo Bosworth joined the cast of The Hills in season 3! She made her first appearance during a New Year's Eve party at Lauren's condo, which slowly but surely led to her gaining a permanent spot on the show. Lo also appeared on Laguna Beach alongside LC, making it clear that she was no stranger to reality TV.

    Related: 'The Hills' Starts Filming Again With A Cast Party And BTS Pics

    Despite her and Lauren's close friendship, the duo is no longer as close as they once were. As for Lo, the star has no interest in ever appearing on reality TV again and has since launched her very own business, Love Wellness, which is taking up most of the star's time, now.

    5 Stephanie Pratt

    Stephanie Pratt, much like a co-star, Lo Bosworth, also joined the cast come the third season. Stephanie is known mainly for being Spencer Pratt's younger sister, however, the two have had quite a rocky relationship. Since The Hills, Stephanie moved over to England, where she became a lead character on the British reality series, Made In Chelsea. Pratt moved back to Los Angeles in order to film for New Beginnings and continued to travel back and forth from Europe to the United States, balancing both shows.

    4 Brody Jenner

    Brody Jenner has been on our radar ever since he first appears on The Hills. The heartthrob was introduced as a possible love interest for Lauren Conrad, however, he quickly became a fan favorite and part of the crew very fast. Since his time on the show, Brody married Kaitlynn Carter, with whom he appeared on the show's reboot. Although Brody and Kaitlynn appeared to be happy, the duo called it quits in August of 2019. Brody is also prepping for the upcoming season of The Hills: New Beginnings, and teased fans with a quick behind the scenes selfie of what's to come.

    3 Kristin Cavallari

    Kristin Cavallari first began her reality TV career alongside Lauren Conrad and Lo Bosworth on MTV's, Laguna Beach. Cavallari replaced LC when she left the show, bringing in a new slate of drama. Since then, Cavallari married former NFL star, Jay Cutler.

    Related: Lauren Conrad Was “Broke” Before ‘The Hills’, Here’s How Much She’s Worth Today

    The two lived in Nashville, Tennessee, where they raised their three kids. In 2020, Kristin revealed that she and Jay would officially be splitting up. During this time, Cavallari launched her own company, Uncommon James, which has gone on to do incredibly well!

    2 Kelly Cutrone

    Kelly Cutrone made her first appearance on The Hills back in season 1 when LC had to score two extra fashion show tickets for Vogue editors. Cutrone was later invited back for season 3 as Lauren and Whitney's boss at her PR firm, People's Revolution. Kelly later joined Port in her New York City-based show, The City.

    In addition to appearing on an array of reality shows all while running a multi-million dollar company, Kelly became a New York Times best-selling author with her books, If You Have To Cry Go Outside, and Normal Gets You Nowhere. The star then went on to appear as a judge on America's Next Top Model. While she'd have her on-screen success, Kelly has taken a step back from the limelight and is focusing on her ever-growing business.

    1 Justin Bobby

    Justin Bobby, who goes by both Justin or Bobby, first joined The Hills back in 2007 for its third season. He instantly became the show's bad-boy who immediately took a liking to Audrina Patridge. While he and Patridge shared a volatile relationship for the remainder of the show, Justin took a break from reality TV and romance after wrapping things up on The Hills. Since then, the star has returned for the reboot, however, during the time in between, Justin Bobby opened his very own salon, Brush Your Hair, and launched a line of products under the same name.
